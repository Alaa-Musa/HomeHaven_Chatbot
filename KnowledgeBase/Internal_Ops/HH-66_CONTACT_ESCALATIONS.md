# Support Escalations — When to Contact Us Urgently (HH-66)

## 1. Overview
While most inquiries are handled through our standard **Tier 1 Support (HH-01)**, certain critical scenarios require immediate intervention. Following this guideline ensures that high-priority issues—such as missing high-value appliances or transit damage—are fast-tracked for resolution.

## 2. Urgent Escalation Triggers
Contact the HomeHaven Support Team immediately if you encounter any of the following:

### Missing "Delivered" Shipments
*   **Scenario:** Your tracking status shows "Delivered," but the item is not at your doorstep, with security, or at the reception.
*   **Action:** Report within **4 hours** of the notification so we can GPS-track the courier’s last position.

### Transit Damage (High-Value Items)
*   **Scenario:** Your **PureBlend PB-900** or **AirPure AP-400** arrives with visible structural damage or a broken seal.
*   **Window:** You must report this within **24 hours** of receipt to remain eligible for an immediate "No-Questions-Asked" replacement.

### Critical Address Corrections
*   **Scenario:** You noticed a typo in your villa number or street name after checkout.
*   **Window:** Corrections must be submitted within **2 hours** of order placement. Once a parcel moves to "Out for Delivery" status, address redirects may incur a re-delivery fee.

## 3. Required Documentation
To accelerate your Tier 2 or Tier 3 escalation, please provide:
*   **Order Identifier:** Your 10-digit Order Number (e.g., HH-XXXX-XXXX).
*   **Visual Evidence:** High-resolution photos of the outer packaging, the shipping label, and the specific damage to the product.
*   **Verified Contact:** The mobile number linked to your **HavenPlus** account for a direct callback from a Specialist.

## 4. Support Availability
Our Dubai-based Customer Care Center operates on Gulf Standard Time (GST):
*   **Daily Operating Hours:** 09:00 – 21:00 (Including Weekends).
*   **Live Chat:** Accessible via the HomeHaven App and Web portal for the fastest response.
*   **Phone Support:** Available for complex logistics or technical installation crises.
*   **Email:** `urgent@homehaven.ae` (Monitored for high-priority triggers only).

## 5. After-Hours Protocol
If an incident occurs outside of 09:00–21:00, please submit your claim via the **In-App Support Ticket** system. Your timestamped submission will preserve your 24-hour reporting window for damaged goods.
