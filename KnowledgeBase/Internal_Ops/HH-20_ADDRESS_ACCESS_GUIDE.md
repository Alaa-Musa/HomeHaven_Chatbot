## Address tips
To reduce missed deliveries:
- Provide a complete **physical address** (no P.O. boxes)
- Include building name, apartment/villa number, and nearest landmark
- Provide a reachable phone number

## Building access
- If your building requires security clearance or a gate pass, arrange it before the delivery window.
- For bulky items, book lift access if required.

## Missed delivery
If the courier cannot reach you, re-delivery may incur **AED 20** (see Delivery & Shipping Guidelines).
