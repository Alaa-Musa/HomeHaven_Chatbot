version: 1.0
last_updated: 2026-01-19

Before the technician arrives, ensure:
- 60cm cabinet opening available
- Grounded power outlet within reach
- Accessible water inlet shut-off valve
- Drain connection point ready
- Clear pathway and access permissions arranged

If these are missing, installation may be postponed and a revisit fee may apply.
