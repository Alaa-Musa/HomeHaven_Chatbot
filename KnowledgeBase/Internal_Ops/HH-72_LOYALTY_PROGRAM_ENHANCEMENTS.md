## Vision
Enhance HavenPlus with richer personalization, more flexible redemption options, and stronger integration with store experiences.

## Key Initiatives
- Tiered promotions tailored to engagement and purchase history.
- Cross-channel redemption (in-store, online, app).
- Real-time points balance on checkout and product detail sheets.

## Privacy & Compliance
- Ensure all enhancements comply with existing data protection policies (HH-14, HH-39).
- Provide opt-out controls for new promotional features.

## Timeline
- Q2 2026: Design and pilot new redemption options.
- Q3 2026: Expand to all stores; update app interfaces.
