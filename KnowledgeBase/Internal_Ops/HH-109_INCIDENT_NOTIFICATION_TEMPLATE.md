## 1. Overview
Standardized communication templates ensure consistent, clear, and legally-vetted messaging during critical events. Use these blocks to maintain brand trust while providing necessary technical or safety information.

## 2. Notification Templates

### A. General Service Disruption (App/Web)
**Subject:** We’re working on it: [System/Service] is currently down

**Body:**
> "Hi [Customer Name], 
>
> We are currently experiencing technical difficulties with our [System, e.g., Checkout/Order Tracking]. Our engineering team is on the case and working to restore full service as quickly as possible.
> 
> **What this means for you:** [Specific Impact, e.g., You may be unable to complete purchases].
> 
> We apologize for the hiccup. You can monitor live updates here: [Link to Status Page]. Thank you for your patience."

### B. Product Recall (Safety/Quality)
**Subject:** IMPORTANT: Safety Recall Notice regarding [Product Name]

**Body:**
> "Dear [Customer Name],
>
> Our records show you purchased the [Product Name] on [Date]. We are reaching out to inform you of a voluntary recall initiated due to [Reason, e.g., a potential structural defect].
>
> **Action Required:** Please stop using the product immediately. 
> 
> **Resolution:** You are eligible for a [Full Refund/Free Replacement]. Please follow this link [Link] to schedule a pickup or visit any retail location. Your safety is our top priority."

### C. Policy Update (Terms of Service/Privacy)
**Subject:** Updates to our [Policy Name]

**Body:**
> "Hello,
>
> We are updating our [Policy Name] to better serve you. These changes will take effect on [Date]. 
>
> **Key Changes include:**
> * [Change 1, e.g., Revised shipping timelines]
> * [Change 2, e.g., Enhanced data encryption protocols]
>
> By continuing to use our services after [Date], you agree to these updated terms. You can read the full document here: [Link]."

## 3. Communication Channels
*   **Critical (Recalls):** Email + SMS + Physical Mail.
*   **Operational (Outages):** In-app banner + Social Media + Status Page.
*   **Administrative (Policy):** Email + Website Footer update.
