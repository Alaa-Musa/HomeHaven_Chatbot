## Objective
Provide a clear, fast, and customer-friendly in-store returns process that aligns with online policies (HH-04) while ensuring proper inspection and restocking practices.

## Workflow
1) Customer presents item with packaging and receipt.
2) Scan order to verify eligibility (within 30 days; items unused; original packaging present).
3) Inspect item for wear, missing parts, and serial number match (per HH-04 and HH-22).
4) Determine refund type (card refund, store credit, or exchange) based on policy and product category.
5) Process return through POS system; issue receipt or store credit; update inventory accordingly.
6) If item is defective, initiate warranty flow (HH-05) where applicable.

## In-Store Documentation
- Maintain a dedicated returns folder for each location with templates for: receipt, serial capture, photos, and notes.
- Use the same photos and notes demanded for courier-based damage claims (HH-21).

## Customer Communication
- Confirm the item’s condition and reason for return.
- Explain estimated processing time for refunds (5–12 business days for card; faster for store credits depending on payment method).

## Exceptions
- Opened consumables are generally restricted (HH-04) unless defective.
- Bundled items may require returning the entire bundle (HH-43).

## Security & Fraud
- Verify customer identity if needed by policy; avoid processing refunds to third parties.

## KPIs
- In-store return processing time (target ≤ 6–8 minutes per item)
- Return accuracy rate (target ≥ 98%)
