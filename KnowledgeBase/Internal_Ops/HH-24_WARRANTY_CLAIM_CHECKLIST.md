# Warranty Claim Checklist: What to Provide (HH-24)

## 1. Overview
To expedite the technical review of your **HomeHaven** product, please ensure all required data is prepared before initiating a claim. Providing a complete "Evidence Package" allows our Service Team to accurately diagnose issues—such as motor failure in the **PB-900** or sensor errors in the **AP-400**—without unnecessary back-and-forth.

## 2. Evidence Submission Requirements
When contacting Support for a warranty-related concern, please include the following:

*   **Transaction ID:** Your 10-digit Order Number (e.g., HH-XXXX-XXXX).
*   **Product Identity:** Full product name and model number (found on the base plate or original box).
*   **Unique Identifier:** The **Serial Number (S/N)**, typically found on a silver or white sticker on the underside or rear of the unit.
*   **Problem Statement:** A brief description of the malfunction (e.g., "Unit powers on but blades do not rotate").
*   **Visual Documentation:** 
    *   **Photos:** Clear shots of the unit and any visible physical defects.
    *   **Video:** A 10-15 second clip demonstrating the issue (e.g., unusual grinding noises or flashing error lights).
*   **Diagnostic Data:** Any specific **Error Codes** appearing on digital displays (e.g., "E1" on the **CC-T1 Thermostat**).

## 3. Post-Submission Workflow
Once your claim is logged, the following steps occur:

1.  **Remote Troubleshooting:** A specialist will provide 1–2 immediate steps to attempt a reset or recalibration.
2.  **Logistics Coordination:** If troubleshooting fails, HomeHaven will coordinate a **Service Center Drop-off** at our Al Quoz hub or a **Courier Collection** for bulky items.
3.  **Technical Inspection:** Our certified technicians will open the unit to verify the internal fault and check for "Misuse" or "Unauthorized Repair" (refer to **HH-51**).

## 4. Service Level Targets (SLA)
*   **Initial Response:** Within **24 business hours** of submission.
*   **Standard Resolution:** Typically **7–14 business days** from the date the item reaches our Service Center.
*   **Extended Timeline:** If proprietary spare parts (e.g., a specific HVAC relay for the **CC-T1**) must be imported, the timeline may be extended. You will receive a weekly status update via SMS.
