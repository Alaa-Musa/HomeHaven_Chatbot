# Marketing Material — Content Creation Guidelines

## Purpose
This policy establishes standards for creating marketing content that aligns with the company’s brand identity, ensures legal compliance, and delivers a consistent message to customers across all channels.

---

## Scope
This policy applies to:

- Digital marketing content (social media, email campaigns, website banners)
- Print marketing materials (flyers, catalogs, posters)
- In-store promotional displays
- Product advertisements and promotional videos
- Content created by internal teams or external agencies on behalf of the company

---

## Objectives
- Maintain consistent brand identity across all marketing materials.
- Ensure all content complies with legal, regulatory, and privacy requirements.
- Promote clear, accurate, and responsible messaging to customers.
- Protect the company’s reputation and intellectual property.

---

## Brand Consistency

### Approved Imagery and Copy
- Use only images, graphics, and copy approved by the Marketing or Brand team.
- Adhere to the company’s brand guidelines, including colors, fonts, logos, and tone of voice.
- Avoid using unlicensed or third-party content without proper permissions.
- Ensure visual and textual elements reflect the company’s brand values.

### Legal Disclosures and Compliance
- Include necessary legal disclaimers, terms, and conditions where applicable.
- Follow data privacy rules (e.g., GDPR, CCPA) when using customer data in marketing campaigns.
- Ensure claims in marketing materials are accurate and substantiated.
- Avoid misleading statements, exaggerations, or unverified comparisons.

---

## Content Creation Process
1. **Drafting:** Content creators produce initial drafts following brand and legal guidelines.
2. **Review:** Marketing and Legal teams review content for accuracy, compliance, and brand adherence.
3. **Approval:** Only content that has received formal approval may be published or distributed.
4. **Publishing:** Distribute content across approved channels in accordance with the marketing plan.
5. **Monitoring:** Track performance and feedback to ensure messaging aligns with brand objectives.

---

## Roles and Responsibilities

### Marketing Team
- Maintain brand guidelines and approved content libraries.
- Review and approve content for consistency and quality.
- Ensure all marketing campaigns comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

### Legal/Compliance Team
- Review marketing materials for compliance with laws, regulations, and data protection standards.
- Advise marketing teams on disclaimers, disclosures, and risk mitigation.

### Content Creators / Agencies
- Follow brand and legal guidelines when producing content.
- Submit materials for review and approval before publishing.

---

## Policy Review
This policy should be reviewed at least annually or after significant updates to brand guidelines, legal regulations, or marketing channels.
