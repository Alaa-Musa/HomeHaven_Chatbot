# Customer Support Channels & What to Include (HH-30)

## 1. Overview
At **HomeHaven**, we aim for a "First-Contact Resolution" (FCR). To ensure our specialists can diagnose your **PureBlend** motor issues or track your **CrispWave** delivery without delay, please use the designated channels and provide the required technical context.

## 2. Support Hours
Our UAE-based support team is available during peak household activity hours:
*   **Daily Operating Window:** 09:00 – 21:00 (Gulf Standard Time).
*   **Response Target:** 
    *   **Chat:** < 5 minutes.
    *   **Phone:** < 2 minutes.
    *   **Email:** < 4 hours.

## 3. Communication Channels

### Live Chat (Recommended)
*   **Access:** Via the "Help" icon in the **HomeHaven App** or web portal.
*   **Best For:** Real-time troubleshooting for the **CC-T1 Thermostat**, quick order status updates, and **HavenPlus** point balance queries.

### Phone Support
*   **Direct Line:** 800-HAVEN (800-42836).
*   **Best For:** Urgent delivery redirects (HH-66), reporting transit damage, or complex installation coordination for bulky items.

### Email Support
*   **Address:** `support@homehaven.ae`.
*   **Best For:** Submitting formal **Warranty Claims (HH-24)**, sending high-resolution photo evidence, or requesting data deletion under our **Privacy Policy (HH-64)**.

## 4. Mandatory Information Checklist
To bypass initial verification and speed up your resolution, please include the following in your initial message:

*   **Order Identifier:** Your 10-digit Order Number (e.g., HH-1234567890).
*   **Product Specifics:** The exact model (e.g., **AirPure AP-400**) and the **Serial Number (S/N)** located on the base of the unit.
*   **Visual Documentation:** Clear photos of the shipping label, damaged packaging, or a short video of a mechanical fault (e.g., unusual noises).
*   **Problem Timeline:** A concise description of when the issue started (e.g., "Immediately after the first 60-second cycle").

## 5. After-Hours Support
If you require assistance outside of 09:00–21:00, please submit a **Support Ticket** through the app. Our system will timestamp your request, ensuring you stay within the **24-hour reporting window** for damaged goods (HH-21), and a specialist will prioritize your case at 09:00 the following morning.
