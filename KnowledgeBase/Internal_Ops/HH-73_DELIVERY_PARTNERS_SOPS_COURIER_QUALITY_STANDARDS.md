## Objective
Ensure consistent courier performance, safe handling of items, and transparent communication with customers.

## Quality Metrics
- On-time delivery rate
- Condition of delivered items (no damage)
- Customer updates and ETA accuracy

## Operational Guidelines
- Verify recipient identity when required; obtain signed delivery notes.
- Handle bulky items with care; ensure safe loading and unloading.
- Notify customers of any delays or issues promptly.

## Incident Handling
- If a delivery exception occurs, escalate to the appropriate channel and log the incident.
- Provide proactive resolution (reschedule, reattempt) within the service window.
