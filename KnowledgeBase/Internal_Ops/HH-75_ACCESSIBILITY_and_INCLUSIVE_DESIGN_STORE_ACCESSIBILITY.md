## Accessibility Principles
- Clear, high-contrast signage; large font sizes; tactile floor indicators.
- Accessible checkout options for customers with disabilities.
- Adequate seating and rest areas near product demonstrations.

## Operational Practices
- Staff trained to assist customers with mobility or sensory disabilities.
- Clear pathways and unobstructed aisles; regular accessibility audits.

## Feedback
Provide channels for customers to report accessibility concerns; track and address issues promptly.
