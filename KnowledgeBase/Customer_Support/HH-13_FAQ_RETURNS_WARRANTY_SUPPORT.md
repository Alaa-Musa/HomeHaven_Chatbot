## Returns
**Q: What is your returns window?**  
A: Most items can be returned within **30 days** if unused and in original packaging (HH-04).

**Q: Can I return opened consumables like sprays or filters?**  
A: Usually no, unless defective (HH-04).

## Warranty
**Q: How long is the warranty?**  
A: Typically **1 year manufacturer warranty** (HH-05).

**Q: Does warranty cover accidental drops or water damage?**  
A: Usually no (HH-05).

## Support
**Q: What are your support hours?**  
A: **Daily 9:00–21:00 (GST)** (HH-01).

**Q: How fast do you respond?**  
A: Target initial troubleshooting response within **24 hours** (HH-05).
