# Click & Collect — Additional FAQs (HH-29)

## 1. Overview
**Click & Collect** is our fastest fulfillment method, allowing HomeHaven customers to bypass courier timelines by picking up their household essentials directly from our Experience Centers in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, or Sharjah.

## 2. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is Click & Collect free?**  
A: **Yes.** Unlike home delivery for low-value orders, Click & Collect has no minimum spend requirement and zero service fees. It is the most cost-effective way to secure small items like **AP-F400 Filter Packs** or **BB-CM125** accessories.

**Q: When will my order be ready for pickup?**  
A: Typically within **2 hours** of order placement, provided the order is made during standard store operating hours (HH-65). You will receive an automated "Ready for Collection" SMS and Email containing your unique pickup QR code. 
*   *Note: Orders placed within 2 hours of store closing will be ready the following morning by 11:00 AM.*

**Q: How long do you hold my order at the store?**  
A: We hold your items for a maximum of **3 calendar days** from the moment the "Ready for Collection" notification is sent. 
*   **Unclaimed Orders:** After 72 hours, the order is automatically cancelled, the items are returned to the shelf, and a refund is processed (minus a 5% restocking fee).

**Q: Can I switch from Home Delivery to Click & Collect after I’ve placed my order?**  
A: **Sometimes.** You must contact **Customer Support (HH-66)** within 30 minutes of order confirmation. Once an order has been "Picked and Packed" at the Al Quoz hub for courier dispatch, it is moving through an automated logistics chain and the method cannot be changed.

**Q: What do I need to bring to the store?**  
A: To prevent identity fraud and ensure your **HavenPlus** points are secured, you must present:
1.  The **Order QR Code** (Digital or Printed).
2.  A valid **Emirates ID** or Passport matching the name on the order.

**Q: Can someone else pick up the order for me?**  
A: Yes. You must forward the Order QR Code to them. They will also need to show a copy of your Emirates ID (digital is fine) to our store associate for verification.

## 3. Location-Specific Notes
*   **Al Quoz (Dubai):** Use the dedicated "Click & Collect" parking bays near the main entrance for rapid loading of bulky items like the **CrispWave CW-AF55**.
*   **Khalidiya (Abu Dhabi):** During peak weekend hours, please allow an additional 15 minutes for curbside assistance.
