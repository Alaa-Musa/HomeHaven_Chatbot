# CrispWave CW-AF55 — Quick Cooking Tips (HH-49)

## 1. Overview
The **CrispWave CW-AF55** utilizes 360° Rapid Air Technology. Following these specialized tips ensures optimal texture, flavor, and longevity of your appliance in a high-performance household setting.

## 2. Professional Cooking Techniques
*   **Preheating Protocol:** Always preheat the unit at 200°C for 3–5 minutes. A hot basket ensures immediate searing of proteins and superior "crisp" on frozen goods.
*   **Aeration Spacing:** Avoid overfilling the basket. For meat and poultry, maintain a single layer with at least 1cm of space between items to allow unobstructed high-velocity airflow.
*   **The "Mid-Cycle Shake":** For fries, nuggets, or roasted vegetables, remove and shake the basket halfway through the timer. This redistributes oils and prevents "soft spots" caused by steam trapped between layers.
*   **Dry to Fry:** Pat proteins (like chicken skin or fish) completely dry with a paper towel before seasoning. Any surface moisture will create steam instead of a crisp crust.

## 3. Recommended Settings for Common Items

| Item | Temperature | Time | Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Home Fries** | 200°C | 15–20 mins | Soak in cold water for 30 mins first to remove starch. |
| **Chicken Wings** | 180°C | 12–15 mins | Spray lightly with oil for a "deep-fried" finish. |
| **Vegetables** | 190°C | 8–10 mins | Toss in a bowl with seasoning before adding to the basket. |
| **Frozen Snacks** | 200°C | 6–10 mins | No extra oil required. |

## 4. Maintenance & Care
*   **Thermal Shock Prevention:** Always let the basket cool to room temperature before exposing it to water. Sudden temperature shifts can cause the metal to warp or the non-stick coating to flake.
*   **Manual Cleaning:** While the basket may be labeled dishwasher-safe, **hand washing** with a soft sponge and non-abrasive detergent is strongly recommended to preserve the premium non-stick surface for the long term.
*   **Heating Element Care:** Periodically check the top heating coil for grease splatter. If dirty, wipe with a damp cloth only once the unit is completely unplugged and cold.

## 5. Safety Notes
*   **External Clearances:** Maintain at least 15cm of open space behind the exhaust vent during operation to prevent overheating and wall discoloration.
*   **Paper Liners:** Never add parchment paper or liners while preheating; the air suction can pull them into the heating element, posing a fire risk.
