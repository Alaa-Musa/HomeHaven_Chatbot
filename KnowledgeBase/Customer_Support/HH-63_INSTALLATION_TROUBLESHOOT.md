# Installation Day — Common Questions (HH-63)

## 1. Overview
To ensure a smooth service experience, this FAQ clarifies the scope of HomeHaven’s professional installation services. Our technicians are specialized in product integration and testing but operate within strict "Standard Installation" boundaries to ensure safety and compliance with UAE residential codes.

## 2. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do you perform carpentry or new plumbing for dishwasher/washing machine installs?**  
A: **No.** Standard installation (e.g., for the DW60) includes connecting the unit to your existing water inlet, drain point, and power socket. It does **not** include:
*   Cutting or modifying kitchen cabinetry/granite countertops.
*   Extending plumbing pipes or installing new drainage traps.
*   Running new electrical conduits or installing new breakers.

**Q: Can you fix my door if the smart lock installation leaves marks or holes from the previous lock?**  
A: **No.** Our service covers the mechanical fitment and functional testing of the smart lock (e.g., SN-L2). Any cosmetic repairs, wood filling, or painting required to cover marks left by your old hardware are the responsibility of the homeowner.

**Q: Will you set up the Wi-Fi and app configuration for my smart devices?**  
A: **Partial Support.** 
*   **Hardware Setup:** Our technician will ensure the device is powered and functioning mechanically.
*   **Connectivity:** For the **SN-L2 Smart Lock**, the primary connection is via **Bluetooth** for local app access. 
*   **Network Troubleshooting:** While we assist with initial pairing, we do not provide IT support for home mesh networks, firewall configurations, or ISP-related connectivity issues.

**Q: What if my existing valves are leaking or the power socket is faulty?**  
A: For safety reasons, our technicians cannot proceed with the installation if the site infrastructure is compromised. You will be advised to contact a licensed plumber or electrician to repair the utilities before we can reschedule the installation (refer to the **Revisit Fee in HH-62**).

**Q: Does the installation include removing my old appliance?**  
A: **Disconnect Only.** We will disconnect your old unit to make space for the new one. However, "Haul-Away" or disposal services must be booked separately as a "Recycling Add-on" at checkout to ensure environmentally friendly disposal.

## 3. Post-Installation Sign-off
Upon completion, the technician will demonstrate the basic functions of the device. You will be asked to sign a **Digital Completion Certificate** confirming:
1.  The unit is level and stable.
2.  There are no visible leaks (for appliances).
3.  The locking mechanism functions correctly (for smart locks).
4.  The work area has been cleared of packaging debris.
