## Customer Support
- **Hours:** Daily 9:00–21:00 (GST)
- **Primary Phone:** 800-HAVEN (800-42836)
- **Live Chat:** Accessible via the HomeHaven App and Web portal

## Department Directory
To ensure the fastest resolution, please direct your inquiry to the appropriate team:

*   **General Support (`support@homehaven.ae`):** Standard inquiries, order status, and HavenPlus rewards.
*   **Urgent Escalations (`urgent@homehaven.ae`):** Missing "Delivered" shipments or address corrections for orders already in transit.
*   **Claims & Damage (`claims@homehaven.ae`):** Submitting photo/video evidence for items damaged during delivery.
*   **Privacy & Compliance (`privacy@homehaven.ae`):** Data access requests, account deletions, and Right to be Forgotten inquiries.
