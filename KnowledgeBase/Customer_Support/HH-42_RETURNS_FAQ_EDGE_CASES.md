**Q: Can I return an item without the free gift included in the box?**  
A: Returns generally require all accessories and free gifts. Missing items may affect eligibility.

**Q: I opened a consumable (spray/filter) and changed my mind. Can I return it?**  
A: Opened consumables are generally restricted unless defective.

**Q: What if the serial number doesn’t match?**  
A: Returns may be refused if serial numbers do not match records.
