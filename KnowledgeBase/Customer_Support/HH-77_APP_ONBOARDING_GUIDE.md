## Onboarding Flow
1) Account creation and verification (phone/email).
2) Privacy & marketing preferences setup.
3) Guided tour of core features: shopping, order tracking, loyalty.
4) Permissions prompts: notifications, location (where applicable).

## Best Practices
- Keep onboarding under 5 minutes.
- Use contextual prompts to guide new users to key actions.
- Provide a clear path to support if users encounter issues.

## Troubleshooting
- If verification fails, offer alternative verification methods.
- If notification delivery fails, provide troubleshooting steps.
