# Delivery — Edge Case FAQs (HH-41)

## 1. Overview
To maintain the precision of our UAE logistics network, HomeHaven strictly enforces cut-off times. This document addresses common "Edge Case" scenarios where order timing or location may shift your expected delivery window.

## 2. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: I placed an order at 6:30 PM in Dubai. Is it still eligible for Next-Day delivery?**  
A: **No.** Our Standard Next-Day service for major cities (Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah) requires orders to be finalized by the **6:00 PM cut-off**. Orders placed at 6:30 PM will be processed the following morning and scheduled for delivery on the next available day (typically 48 hours from the original order time).

**Q: I placed an Express order at 1:00 PM in Abu Dhabi. Can it be delivered same-day?**  
A: **No.** Express same-day delivery in Dubai and Abu Dhabi requires a strict **12:00 PM (Noon) cut-off** to allow for sorting and vehicle routing. Orders placed after 12:00 PM will shift to the next business day’s Express window or can be downgraded to Standard delivery upon request.

**Q: Do you deliver on Sundays?**  
A: Our standard delivery operations run **Monday–Saturday**. Sunday is currently considered a non-operational day for our courier fleet. Any orders placed over the weekend (after Saturday 6:00 PM) will be dispatched on Monday for Tuesday arrival.

**Q: What happens if I live in a "Remote Area" like Western Region (Ghayathi) or Hatta?**  
A: Remote areas do not follow the next-day major city rule. Deliveries to these locations typically occur on a **fixed weekly schedule** (e.g., Tuesdays and Thursdays). Please allow **3–5 business days** for these coordinates.

**Q: My building has a 5:00 PM noise restriction for bulky items. Can you still deliver my dishwasher in the Evening Slot?**  
A: If your building management prohibits "Major Appliance" movement after 5:00 PM, you must select the **Morning (10:00–14:00)** or **Afternoon (14:00–18:00)** slots. If our team is turned away by security due to community rules, a **re-delivery fee (HH-54)** will apply.

## 3. Pro-Tips for Fast Fulfillment
*   **The "Golden Hour":** Place orders before 4:00 PM to ensure ample time for payment verification and warehouse picking before the final 6:00 PM truck departure.
*   **Payment Speed:** Orders paid via Credit Card or Apple Pay are verified instantly. **Cash on Delivery (COD)** orders may require a brief phone verification, which could push your order past the cut-off if you are unreachable.
