# Privacy — Data Retention Q&A (HH-64)

## 1. Overview
At **HomeHaven**, we take the stewardship of your personal information seriously. This Q&A clarifies how we handle your data throughout its lifecycle, from the moment you place an order for a kitchen appliance to the long-term management of your account history.

## 2. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do you keep my order history indefinitely?**  
A: **Yes.** We retain order records (including product details, transaction amounts, and delivery addresses) to fulfill our business and legal obligations. This data is essential for:
*   **Warranty Verification:** Ensuring we can service your products throughout their lifecycle.
*   **Tax Compliance:** Meeting UAE Federal Tax Authority (FTA) requirements for financial record-keeping.
*   **Recall Notifications:** Contacting you immediately if a safety issue is identified with a product you purchased.

**Q: Do you sell my personal data to third parties?**  
A: **No.** HomeHaven does not sell your personal information for profit. Data sharing is strictly limited to essential service providers required to fulfill your order, including:
*   **Logistics Partners:** To navigate to your physical address for delivery.
*   **Payment Processors:** To securely authorize your credit card or Tabby/Tamara transactions.
*   **Technical Support:** To troubleshoot smart-home connectivity issues (e.g., for the CC-T1 Thermostat).

**Q: Can I opt out of marketing communications?**  
A: **Absolutely.** You have full control over your communication preferences. You can opt out via:
*   **Email:** Clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any promotional message.
*   **App Settings:** Toggling "Push Notifications" or "Marketing Emails" in the HavenPlus Rewards dashboard.
*   **Customer Support:** Contacting us directly to be placed on our internal "Do Not Contact" (DNC) list.
*   *Note: You will still receive transactional emails, such as order confirmations and shipping alerts.*

**Q: How long do you keep my account data if I stop shopping?**  
A: If an account remains inactive for **3 years**, we move the profile to an "Archived" state. After **5 years** of total inactivity, personal identifiers are anonymized, though raw transaction data is kept for aggregate financial reporting.

## 3. Data Protection Rights
In line with UAE Data Protection laws, you may request:
1.  **Access:** A digital copy of all personal data we hold about you.
2.  **Correction:** Updates to incorrect phone numbers or delivery addresses.
3.  **Deletion:** Permanent removal of your account, provided there are no active warranties or legal holds.
