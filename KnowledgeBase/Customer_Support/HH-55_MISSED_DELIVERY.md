# Missed Delivery — What Happens Next (HH-55)

## 1. Overview
At **HomeHaven**, we strive to get your household essentials to you on the first try. However, when a delivery attempt fails due to customer unavailability or access issues, the following protocols apply to ensure your items are eventually secured.

## 2. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What happens if I miss my delivery?**  
A: Our courier partner will attempt to contact you via the phone number provided at checkout. If they cannot reach you or gain access to the premises (e.g., restricted villa gate or no building security clearance), the shipment will be returned to our local sorting hub. 

**Q: Is there a fee for re-delivery?**  
A: For standard parcels, the first re-delivery attempt is free. Subsequent attempts or re-scheduling for bulky/installed items (after a "No-Show") will incur a flat **AED 20** re-delivery fee to cover additional logistics costs.

**Q: How can I avoid a missed delivery?**  
A: 
*   **Be Reachable:** Ensure your primary UAE mobile number is active and the ringer is on during your selected window.
*   **Permits:** Arrange all necessary **Move-In Permits (MIP)** or Gate Passes with your building management (especially in communities like Downtown Dubai, Marina, or Reem Island).
*   **Alternative Drop-off:** If you won't be home, you can authorize the courier via SMS to leave the package with "Building Security" or "Reception" (at your own risk).

**Q: Can I change my delivery address last minute?**  
A: Address changes can be made up to **12 hours** before the delivery window starts. Once the vehicle has departed the Al Quoz warehouse, any change of location will result in a failed attempt and a scheduled re-delivery for the following day.

## 3. Storage Policy
*   **Hold Period:** If we are unable to reach you for **5 consecutive days**, the order will be automatically cancelled.
*   **Refunds on Cancellation:** A refund will be processed back to your original payment method, minus any applicable shipping or "No-Show" fees.

## 4. Tracking Your Driver
You can view the real-time status of your delivery via the **HomeHaven App**. Once the driver is "En Route," you will receive a direct link to track their GPS location and see your estimated "Stops Away."
