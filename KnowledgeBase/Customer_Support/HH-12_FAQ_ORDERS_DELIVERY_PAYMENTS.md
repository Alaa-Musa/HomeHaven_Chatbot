## Orders
**Q: Can I change my delivery address after ordering?**  
A: Sometimes. Contact support as soon as possible. If the courier has already picked up the parcel, changes may not be possible.

**Q: Do you deliver to P.O. boxes?**  
A: No. We require a physical address (see HH-02).

## Delivery
**Q: What is the free delivery threshold?**  
A: Free standard delivery on orders **AED 199+** (HH-02).

**Q: Do you offer same-day delivery?**  
A: Yes—Express delivery for **Dubai and Abu Dhabi** if ordered by **12:00pm**, fee **AED 35** (HH-02).

**Q: What if my item arrives damaged?**  
A: Report within **24 hours** with photos of packaging and damage (HH-02).

## Payments
**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: Card payments (Visa/Mastercard), Apple Pay (supported devices), and Cash on Delivery (selected areas).

**Q: Do you offer installment plans?**  
A: Yes, we offer interest-free installments via Tabby and Tamara for qualifying orders.

## Click & Collect
**Q: How long do you hold Click & Collect orders?**  
A: **3 days** from readiness notification (HH-03).
