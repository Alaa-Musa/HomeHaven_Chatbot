## What we may collect
- Device/app identifiers
- Basic usage analytics (e.g., screens viewed, taps)
- Fraud-prevention signals

## Why we collect it
- Improve the app experience
- Detect suspicious transactions
- Fix bugs and measure performance

## Marketing choices
You can opt out of marketing in app settings or via unsubscribe links.
