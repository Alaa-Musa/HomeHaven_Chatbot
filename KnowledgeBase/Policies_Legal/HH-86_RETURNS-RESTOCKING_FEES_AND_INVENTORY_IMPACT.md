# Returns — Restocking Fees & Inventory Impact (HH-86)

## 1. Overview
To maintain the highest quality standards for our UAE customers while managing the high logistics costs of household appliances, HomeHaven applies a restocking and processing framework. This policy ensures that returned inventory is handled sustainably and that operational costs for non-defective returns are shared fairly.

## 2. Restocking Fees
Restocking fees are applied to "Change-of-Mind" returns where the product is non-defective but requires significant labor for inspection and repackaging.

*   **Standard Restocking Fee:** A 5% fee applies specifically to unclaimed Click & Collect orders, while a 10% fee applies to standard change-of-mind courier returns where the box was opened.. The precentage of the item’s retail value** is deducted from the refund for opened but unused items (e.g., a **CrispWave CW-AF55** where the box was opened but the unit was never powered on).
*   **Unclaimed Click & Collect:** As per **HH-29**, a **5% restocking fee** applies to orders not collected within the 3-day holding window.
*   **Exemptions:** No restocking fees apply if:
    *   The item is confirmed as **Defective** by a HomeHaven technician.
    *   The item was **Damaged in Transit** (reported within 24 hours per **HH-21**).
    *   The wrong model was shipped due to a HomeHaven fulfillment error.

## 3. Inventory Impact & Inspection Grades
Every returned item undergoes a multi-point inspection at our Al Quoz hub to determine its next lifecycle stage:

*   **Grade A (Resellable):** Item is in pristine condition with all original internal packaging, manuals, and accessories. The box seal may be broken, but the product is untouched.
    *   *Action:* Sanitized, resealed with "Inspected by HomeHaven" tape, and returned to active inventory.
*   **Grade B (Open-Box / Outlet):** Item is functional but shows minor cosmetic handling marks or the original box is moderately damaged.
    *   *Action:* Transferred to the **HomeHaven Outlet** section at a 20–30% discount. These items carry a limited 6-month warranty.
*   **Grade C (Damaged / Incomplete):** Item has missing parts, visible scratches, or shows signs of actual use (e.g., food residue in a blender).
    *   *Action:* Sent to the **Service Center** for parts harvesting or professional refurbishment.

## 4. Non-Restockable Items & Recycling
*   **Hygiene & Consumables:** Items identified in **HH-23** (filters, detergents, personal care) that have been opened are immediately flagged for **E-Recycling**. They are never returned to inventory for health and safety reasons.
*   **Sustainability Goal:** HomeHaven aims to divert 90% of non-resellable electronic waste from UAE landfills through our partnership with local "Green-Cycle" facilities.

## 5. Refund Deduction Process
When a return is approved, the **Restocking Fee** and any **Courier Pickup Fees (HH-52)** are automatically calculated and deducted from the final credit. Your "Refund Summary" email will provide a transparent line-item breakdown of these deductions.
