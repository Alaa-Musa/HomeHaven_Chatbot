## Warranty basics
Most electrical products include a **1-year manufacturer warranty** starting from:
- Delivery date (delivery orders), or
- Pickup date (Click & Collect)

## What warranty covers
- Manufacturing defects
- Faults under normal household use

## What warranty usually does NOT cover
- Accidental damage, drops, cracks
- Liquid damage (unless explicitly rated and used correctly)
- Wear-and-tear parts (filters, brush rolls, batteries that degrade over time)
- Damage from voltage issues or improper installation
- Unauthorized repair or modification

## How to claim warranty
1) Contact HomeHaven support with:
   - Order number
   - Product name/model
   - Short description + photos/video
2) We’ll troubleshoot first.
3) If needed, we arrange:
   - Service center drop-off, or
   - Courier collection (depending on product category)

## Repair, replacement, or refund
Outcome depends on service assessment:
- Repair (most common)
- Replacement (if repair not feasible and stock available)
- Refund (if replacement not available)

## Service Level Target (informal)
- Initial troubleshooting response: within **24 hours**
- Typical service resolution: **7–14 business days**
(Complex parts availability can extend this.)
