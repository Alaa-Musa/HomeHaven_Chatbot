## Recall Triggers
- Manufacturer safety alerts
- Regulatory notices
- Field issues affecting user safety

## Response Protocol
1) Halt distribution of affected items at all stores.
2) Notify customers via marketing channels if they own the product (based on order history).
3) Quarantine affected inventory; document serial ranges.

## Customer Communication
Provide clear instructions for return or inspection; offer replacement or refund per policy.

## Documentation
Maintain a recall log; track affected SKUs, quantities, and locations.
