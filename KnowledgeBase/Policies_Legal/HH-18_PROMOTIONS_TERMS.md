# Promotions & Discount Codes Terms (HH-18)

## 1. Overview
HomeHaven offers promotional codes and seasonal discounts to provide better value on household essentials. This policy ensures fair usage and transparency regarding how these discounts interact with our standard pricing, HavenPlus rewards, and service fees.

## 2. General Rules & Eligibility
*   **Item Eligibility:** Promo codes are typically category-specific (e.g., "Kitchen20" for the **PureBlend** or **BrewBuddy** series). They do not apply to "Final Sale" or "Clearance" items unless explicitly stated.
*   **Service Exclusions:** Unless a promotion specifically mentions "Free Installation" or "Free Shipping," discount codes apply only to the retail price of the physical product. They cannot be used to offset:
    *   **Delivery Fees** (Standard or Express).
    *   **Professional Installation Fees** (e.g., for the **SN-L2 Smart Lock**).
    *   **COD Service Fees** (AED 15).
    *   **Extended Warranty Plans**.
*   **Usage Limits:** Most promotional codes are restricted to **one use per customer** and one code per transaction.

## 3. Discount Stacking & HavenPlus
*   **Code Stacking:** Multiple promo codes cannot be combined in a single order. The system will automatically apply the discount with the highest value if multiple are entered.
*   **HavenPlus Interaction:** 
    *   **Points + Promo:** You may typically redeem your **HavenPlus** points (in 500-point increments) alongside a single promotional code, unless the promotion is marked as "Exclusive" or "Non-Combinable."
    *   **Tier Multipliers:** Seasonal "Double Points" promotions are calculated based on the net price paid *after* the discount code is applied.

## 4. Returns & Refund Logic
If a discounted item is returned, the financial reconciliation follows the "Net Paid" principle:
*   **Refund Amount:** You will be refunded the actual amount charged to your card or paid via COD. The "value" of the discount code is not refundable or transferable to a future order.
*   **Partial Returns:** If a promo code required a minimum spend (e.g., "AED 50 off AED 500") and a partial return drops your total below that threshold, the discount will be voided, and the difference will be deducted from your refund.

## 5. Misuse & Fraud Prevention
To maintain the integrity of our community offers, HomeHaven monitors for promotional abuse:
*   **Duplicate Accounts:** Creating multiple accounts to reuse a "New Joiner" code is prohibited.
*   **Bulk Resale:** Using promotional codes to purchase items for commercial resale is a violation of our Terms of Service.
*   **Right to Cancel:** HomeHaven reserves the right to deactivate any promo code or cancel associated orders if we suspect technical exploitation or fraudulent acquisition of codes.

## 6. Expiry & Technical Issues
*   **Validity:** All codes have a strict expiry date (typically 11:59 PM GST on the date of expiry). 
*   **Support:** If a valid code fails to apply to an eligible **AirPure** or **DustDash** product, please contact **Customer Support (HH-30)** with a screenshot of the error before finalizing your payment.
