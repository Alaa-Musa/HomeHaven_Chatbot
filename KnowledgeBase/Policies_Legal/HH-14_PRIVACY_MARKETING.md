## What we collect (summary)
- Account details (name, phone, email)
- Delivery address
- Order history and support interactions
- Device/app identifiers (for analytics and fraud prevention)

## How we use it
- To fulfill orders and provide customer support
- To improve product recommendations (optional)
- To send marketing messages if you opt in

## Marketing preferences
You can opt out of marketing at any time:
- Email unsubscribe link, or
- App settings → Notifications, or
- Contact support

## Data retention (simple statement)
We retain order records for business and legal purposes. We aim to minimize access and restrict sharing with third parties to what is needed for delivery and payments.
