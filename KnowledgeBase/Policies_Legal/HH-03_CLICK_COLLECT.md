## Overview
Click & Collect lets you order online and pick up in-store.

## Fees & timing
- **Cost:** Free
- **Ready time:** Typically **within 2 hours** (during store hours)
- **Hold time:** We hold items for **3 days** from “ready for pickup” notification.

## Pickup requirements
Bring:
- Order confirmation (email or app)
- Valid ID matching the order name

## Someone else picking up?
Allowed if you:
- Add their name in the pickup section at checkout (or contact support before pickup)
- They present the order confirmation + their ID

## What if I don’t collect in time?
If not collected within 3 days:
- Order is cancelled automatically
- Refund issued to the original payment method (timing depends on bank)

## Eligible items
Most items are eligible. Some bulky installed items may be delivery-only.
