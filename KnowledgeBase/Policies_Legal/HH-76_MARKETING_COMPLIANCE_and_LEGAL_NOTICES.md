## Advertising Standards
- Truthful claims; no deceptive pricing.
- Clear, legible terms for promotions; explicit exclusions.

## Data & Privacy Notices
- Marketers must respect opt-out preferences (HH-14).
- Collect only necessary data for marketing purposes with proper consent.

## Intellectual Property
- Respect brand and product copyrights; avoid unauthorized use of logos or trademarks.

## Regional Compliance
- Tailor messaging to UAE regulations; include VAT disclosures where appropriate.
