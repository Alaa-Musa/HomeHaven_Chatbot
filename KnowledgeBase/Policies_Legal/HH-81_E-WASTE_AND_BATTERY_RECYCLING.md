# E-Waste & Battery Recycling Programs (HH-81)

## 1. Overview
HomeHaven is committed to reducing the environmental footprint of household technology in the UAE. Our **Green-Cycle Initiative** provides a safe, compliant pathway for customers to dispose of end-of-life electronics, preventing hazardous materials from entering local landfills.

## 2. Program Details & Collection
*   **Primary Hub:** The **Dubai — Al Quoz Experience Center (HH-65)** serves as our central collection point for e-waste.
*   **Accepted Items:** 
    *   **Small Household Electronics:** Defective blenders (**PB-900**), broken steam mops (**SS-M10**), and outdated thermostats.
    *   **Battery Cells:** AA/AAA alkaline batteries and specialized Lithium-Ion packs from the **DustDash** series.
*   **Disposal Guidance:** Store staff are trained to identify recyclable components versus general waste. Customers can also use the "Recycle-Scan" feature in the HomeHaven App to determine if an item is eligible for the drop-box.

## 3. Battery Handling & Safety Protocols
To mitigate fire risks and chemical leaks during transport and storage:
*   **Terminal Protection:** Customers are advised to tape the terminals of high-capacity Lithium batteries (e.g., **DD-BAT2**) with clear packing tape to prevent short-circuits.
*   **Damaged Cells:** Swollen, leaking, or "vented" batteries must be handed directly to a Safety Specialist in-store and should **never** be placed in the general drop-box.
*   **Storage Environment:** Collection bins are fire-rated, climate-controlled, and positioned away from flammable inventory at the Al Quoz hub.

## 4. Community Engagement & Incentives
*   **HavenPlus "Green Points":** Members receive **100 loyalty points** for every small appliance recycled (up to 500 points per month).
*   **Awareness Campaigns:** HomeHaven hosts quarterly "E-Waste Week" events featuring double-points for recycling and live demonstrations on how to extend the life of household batteries.
*   **Educational Content:** Monthly newsletters include "Eco-Tips" on maintaining appliance efficiency to reduce premature e-waste.

## 5. Downstream Processing
*   **Certified Partners:** All collected e-waste is transferred to UAE-based, municipality-approved recycling facilities.
*   **Resource Recovery:** Our partners prioritize the extraction of raw materials (copper, silver, and rare-earth metals) to be reintroduced into the global manufacturing supply chain.
