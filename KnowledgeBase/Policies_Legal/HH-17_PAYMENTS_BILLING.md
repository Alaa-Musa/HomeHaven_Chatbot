# Payments & Billing Policy (HH-17)

## 1. Overview
HomeHaven provides a secure and transparent billing environment for all household transactions. This policy outlines the financial protocols for acquiring our kitchen, cleaning, and smart-home products within the UAE.

## 2. Accepted Payment Methods
To provide maximum flexibility, we support the following payment channels:
*   **Credit/Debit Cards:** All UAE and international Visa and Mastercard are accepted.
*   **Mobile Wallets:** Apple Pay is supported for seamless checkout on compatible iOS and macOS devices.
*   **Cash on Delivery (COD):** Available for orders up to **AED 5,000** in serviceable residential areas. (Note: A non-refundable **AED 15** service fee applies).
*   **Buy Now, Pay Later:** Installment options via Tabby or Tamara are available for qualifying orders at checkout.

## 3. Billing & Authorization
*   **Data Accuracy:** To prevent processing delays, the "Cardholder Name" and "Billing Address" should align with your bank records.
*   **Authorization Holds:** Upon placing an order, a temporary authorization hold may appear on your bank statement. This is a standard verification of funds; the final "Capture" of the payment occurs only once the order is "Ready for Dispatch" from our Al Quoz hub.
*   **Currency:** All transactions are processed in **AED**. International cardholders may be subject to additional currency conversion fees by their issuing bank.

## 4. Cash on Delivery (COD) Protocols
*   **Exact Change:** For the safety and efficiency of our couriers, please prepare the exact order total in AED. Drivers carry minimal change.
*   **Verification:** High-value COD orders (above AED 1,000) may require a one-time phone verification by our support team before the order is released for delivery.
*   **Non-Payment:** Failure to provide payment at the door will result in the order being returned to the hub. A re-delivery fee (HH-55) may apply for subsequent attempts.

## 5. Receipts & Documentation
*   **Tax Invoices:** A formal digital Tax Invoice (including the HomeHaven TRN) is emailed automatically upon order confirmation.
*   **Sustainability:** We do not provide paper receipts with deliveries to reduce environmental waste. You can download your full purchase history from the "My Account" section of the HomeHaven App.

## 6. Fraud Prevention & Security
*   **Verification Requests:** As part of our commitment to secure retail, our Risk Team may contact you via a verified HomeHaven email or phone number to confirm high-value or unusual transactions.
*   **Order Cancellation:** HomeHaven reserves the right to cancel any order that fails our automated fraud-screening protocols or where the customer is unreachable for identity verification.
