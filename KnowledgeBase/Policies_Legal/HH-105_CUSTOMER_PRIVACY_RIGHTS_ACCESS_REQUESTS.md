1. Overview
In accordance with global data protection standards, we grant customers full transparency regarding their personal information. This policy outlines the procedures for customers to exercise their rights to access, rectify, or erase their data stored within our systems.

## Customer Rights

### Access to Personal Data
Right to Know: Customers may request a comprehensive report of the personal data we maintain, including purchase history, contact details, and marketing preferences.
Data Portability: Upon request, data will be provided in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format (e.g., CSV or PDF).

### Corrections and Updates
Accuracy: Customers have the right to request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
Self-Service: Minor updates (address, phone number) should be encouraged through the "My Account" portal for immediate effect.

### Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)
Erasure: Customers may request the permanent deletion of their personal data, provided it is no longer necessary for legal, tax, or active contractual obligations (e.g., open warranty claims).
Anonymization: In cases where transaction data must be kept for financial auditing, personal identifiers will be scrubbed to ensure the record is no longer linkable to the individual.

## Submission and Processing
### How to Submit Requests
Primary Channel: Requests should be submitted via the Privacy Webform located in the "Trust Center" of our website.
Secondary Channel: Verified email requests sent to privacy@homehaven.ae.
Verification: To protect customer security, all requests require identity verification (e.g., two-factor authentication or government-issued ID check) before data is released or deleted.

### Expected Timelines
Acknowledgment: Receipt of the request will be acknowledged within 48 hours.
Resolution: Comprehensive data requests or deletion actions must be completed within 30 calendar days.
Extensions: In complex cases, we reserve the right to extend the deadline by an additional 60 days, provided the customer is notified of the delay and the reason.
