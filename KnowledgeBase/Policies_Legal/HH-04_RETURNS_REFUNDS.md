## Standard returns (most items)
You can return most items within **30 days** of delivery or pickup if:
- Unused
- In original packaging
- Includes all accessories, manuals, and free gifts

### Return methods
1) **Courier pickup** (available in most areas)
2) **In-store return** (Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah stores)

### Return fees
- If the return is due to a defect or our error: **no fee**
- If you changed your mind: pickup fee may apply depending on area (quoted at request time)

## Non-returnable or restricted items
The following are generally not eligible once opened/used:
- Hygiene-related items (e.g., personal care trimmers, oral irrigators)
- Consumables (e.g., water filter cartridges, detergent refills)
- Gift cards and digital codes
- Items marked “Final Sale”

If an item is defective, warranty rules may apply even if opened.

## Refund timelines
- Card payments: typically **5–12 business days** after inspection
- Cash on delivery refunds: bank transfer or store credit (customer choice)

## Return condition checks
We may refuse a refund if:
- Serial number does not match our records
- Missing parts or heavy wear
- Damage caused by misuse, liquid ingress (where applicable), or unauthorized repair

## Bundles
If you return a bundle, you must return all bundle items unless support approves a partial return (rare).
