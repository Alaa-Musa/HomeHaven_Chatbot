# Payment Methods — Regional Variations & Compliance (HH-78)

## 1. Overview
As **HomeHaven** expands its footprint across the Gulf, this policy ensures that our payment infrastructure remains compliant with the distinct financial regulations of each GCC member state while maintaining a localized, frictionless checkout experience for household essentials.

## 2. Regional Payment Availability

### United Arab Emirates (Primary Hub)
*   **Card Payments:** Full support for Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.
*   **Mobile Wallets:** Native integration with **Apple Pay** and **Google Pay** for instant checkout via the HomeHaven App.
*   **Cash on Delivery (COD):** Available for orders up to **AED 5,000** in all major cities. A non-refundable **AED 15** service fee applies.
*   **Installments:** "Buy Now, Pay Later" (BNPL) via Tabby and Tamara for qualifying baskets.

### Other GCC Regions (KSA, Oman, Qatar, Bahrain, Kuwait)
*   **Card Payments:** Support for local debit schemes, including **Mada** (KSA) and **Benefit** (Bahrain).
*   **Cash on Delivery:** Available on a restricted basis for "Non-Bulky" items only. International COD surcharges apply to cover cross-border logistics.
*   **Currency:** Transactions are processed in the local currency (SAR, OMR, QAR, BHD, KWD) based on the daily mid-market exchange rate.

## 3. Financial Compliance & Standards
To protect the integrity of every transaction—from a **PureBlend** purchase to a **HavenPlus** top-up—HomeHaven adheres to:
*   **PCI-DSS Level 1:** Our payment gateway undergoes annual third-party audits to ensure the highest level of Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards. No raw card data is stored on HomeHaven’s internal servers.
*   **Central Bank Regulations:** All digital wallet integrations comply with the UAE Central Bank’s Retail Payment Services and Card Schemes Regulations.
*   **AML/CTF:** Anti-Money Laundering and Counter-Terrorism Financing checks are automatically applied to high-value transactions exceeding **AED 10,000**.

## 4. Security & Fraud Prevention
*   **3D Secure (3DS):** Mandatory 2-factor authentication (OTP via SMS/Email) is required for all online card transactions to reduce the risk of unauthorized use.
*   **Risk Scoring:** Every order is analyzed by an AI-driven fraud engine. Indicators such as "High-Speed Velocity" or "Mismatched IP-to-Shipping Address" may trigger a manual verification call from our **Security Team (HH-83)**.
*   **Tokenization:** We utilize secure tokenization for "Saved Cards," ensuring that even if a local device is compromised, the actual card details remain unreadable.

## 5. Dispute & Chargeback Protocol
*   **Resolution First:** Customers are encouraged to contact **Support (HH-30)** prior to initiating a bank chargeback. Most billing discrepancies can be resolved within 48 hours via a direct refund.
*   **Documentation:** In the event of a formal dispute, HomeHaven provides the issuing bank with a comprehensive "Evidence Pack," including the **Digital Tax Invoice (HH-79)** and the **Proof of Delivery (POD)** signature.
