# Category Exceptions — Hygiene, Consumables, and Final Sale (HH-23)

## 1. Overview
While HomeHaven offers a flexible 30-day return window, certain product categories are subject to restricted return conditions due to health, safety, and hygiene regulations, or their consumable nature. This policy ensures the integrity and safety of the inventory provided to all UAE households.

## 2. Restricted Categories (Non-Returnable Once Opened/Used)

### Hygiene-Related Items
For the protection of our customers, items that come into direct contact with the body or water systems cannot be returned once the factory seal is broken.
*   **Personal Care:** Trimmers, shavers, and hair styling tools.
*   **Health & Wellness:** Oral irrigators (water flossers) and electric toothbrushes.
*   **Note:** If the external shrink-wrap or tamper-evident seal is intact, these items remain eligible for return under **HH-52**.

### Consumables & Liquid Solutions
Products that are depleted through use are ineligible for return once opened.
*   **Cleaning Solutions:** Detergent refills, specialized surface sprays, and floor polishes.
*   **Filtration Media:** Replacement HEPA filters (e.g., **AP-F400**), water pitcher filters, and carbon cartridges.
*   **Kitchen Sundries:** Descaling tablets and coffee-related consumables.

### Final Sale & Clearance
*   **Status:** Items clearly marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance" on the product page or physical price tag.
*   **Condition:** These are sold at significant discounts and are ineligible for change-of-mind returns or exchanges.

## 3. Exceptions for Defective Goods
The restrictions in Section 2 apply strictly to "Change-of-Mind" returns. 
*   **Manufacturing Defects:** If a hygiene-related item fails to power on or a consumable refill is found to be leaking upon arrival, the standard **Warranty (HH-51)** or **Escalation (HH-66)** protocols apply.
*   **Resolution:** In these cases, HomeHaven will provide a replacement or store credit following a technical inspection.

## 4. Pre-Purchase Verification Tip
To avoid disappointment, we strongly recommend the following before breaking a product seal:
*   **Dimensions:** For built-in filters or appliances, double-check the "Compatibility Notes" on the Product Detail Sheet (e.g., **HH-57**).
*   **Model Matching:** Verify that the replacement part number (e.g., **AP-F400**) matches your existing base unit model (e.g., **AP-400**).
*   **Consultation:** When in doubt, use the "Live Chat" feature in the HomeHaven app to confirm compatibility with a specialist.
