# Scheduled Delivery for Bulky / Installed Items (HH-54)

## 1. Overview
To ensure the safe transport and professional setup of large-scale HomeHaven products, we provide specialized **Scheduled Delivery & Installation** services. This policy outlines the logistics framework for items that require two-person handling or specialized technical integration into the home environment.

## 2. Applicability
Scheduled Delivery is mandatory for the following categories:
*   **Major Appliances:** Refrigerators, washing machines, and dryers.
*   **Integrated Units:** Built-in dishwashers, hobs, and ovens requiring cabinetry fitting.
*   **Large Furniture:** Dining sets, sofas, and heavy-duty shelving units.
*   **Smart-Home Systems:** Wall-mounted displays or interconnected security hubs.

## 3. Delivery Slot Options
Customers can select their preferred 4-hour window via the HomeHaven App or through our Logistics Coordinator:
*   **Morning Slot:** 10:00 – 14:00
*   **Afternoon Slot:** 14:00 – 18:00
*   **Evening Slot:** 18:00 – 22:00
*   *Note: Evening slots may be subject to community noise-restriction policies.*

## 4. Pre-Delivery Checklist (Customer Responsibility)
To avoid failed delivery attempts or installation delays, customers must ensure the following:
*   **Building Access:** Secure necessary Move-In Permits (MIP) or Gate Passes from building management/security in advance.
*   **Service Elevator:** Ensure elevator access is available for the selected slot; our teams are not permitted to carry bulky items beyond the second floor via stairs.
*   **Clear Pathway:** Remove rugs, delicate decor, or small furniture that may obstruct the path from the entrance to the designated room.
*   **Utility Readiness:** For appliances, ensure power outlets, water inlets, and drainage points are within 1.5 meters of the installation site and meet UAE electrical standards.

## 5. Rescheduling & No-Show Policy
*   **Cancellations:** Must be made at least **24 hours** prior to the scheduled slot to avoid a re-delivery fee of AED 50.
*   **Late Arrival:** Our team will wait for a maximum of **20 minutes** at the location. If the customer is unreachable, the item will return to the Al Quoz warehouse, and a re-delivery charge will apply.
