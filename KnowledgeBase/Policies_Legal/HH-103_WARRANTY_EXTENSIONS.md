## Overview

Beyond the standard 1-year manufacturer’s warranty, customers may purchase Optional Extended Warranty Plans. These plans provide peace of mind by protecting home furnishings and appliances against mechanical breakdown, accidental damage, and wear and tear for an additional period.

## Terms and Coverage

### Coverage Details
Duration: Extensions are available in 2-year, 3-year, or 5-year increments, beginning upon the expiration of the 1-year standard warranty.
Inclusions: Protection against structural defects, electrical failure in powered furniture (e.g., recliners), and accidental stains or tears (plan-dependent).
Benefits: Includes "No Lemon" guarantees—if a product requires more than three repairs for the same issue, it will be replaced entirely.

### Pricing and Eligibility
Pricing: Calculated as a percentage (typically 10-15%) of the original retail price (MSRP).
Eligibility: Must be purchased at the Point of Sale (POS) or within 30 days of the original delivery date.
Exclusions: Commercial use, cosmetic wear (intentional), or damage caused by unauthorized modifications.

## Claim Process

### Filing a Claim
Notification: Customers must report issues within 14 days of discovery via the Online Warranty Portal.
Documentation: Photos of the defect and the original proof of purchase are required.
Assessment: Remote troubleshooting or a local technician dispatch will be scheduled within 3-5 business days.
### Exclusions & Limitations
Wear and Tear: Normal fading from sunlight or pilling of fabric.
External Factors: Damage from pests, fire, or "Acts of God" (floods/earthquakes).
Unauthorized Repair: Any attempt to repair the item via a third party prior to claim approval voids the extension.
