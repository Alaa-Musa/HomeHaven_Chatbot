## Delivery options

### 1) Standard delivery (UAE)
- **Fee:** Free on orders **AED 199+**
- **Fee (below threshold):** **AED 15**
- **Timeframes:**
  - Major cities (Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah): **Next-day** if ordered by **6:00pm**
  - Other areas: **2–3 business days**

> Business days are Monday–Saturday (Sunday is non-working for deliveries in this scenario).

### 2) Express delivery (selected cities)
Available for **Dubai and Abu Dhabi** only.
- **Fee:** **AED 35**
- **Cut-off:** Order by **12:00pm** for **same-day** delivery
- **Delivery window:** 16:00–22:00

### 3) Scheduled delivery
For bulky or installed items (e.g., dishwashers).
- Choose a date at checkout (subject to availability)
- Slots: 10:00–14:00, 14:00–18:00, 18:00–22:00

## Delivery rules & exceptions
- We do not deliver to P.O. boxes. A physical address and phone number are required.
- Missed delivery: courier will attempt contact; if unsuccessful, re-delivery may incur **AED 20**.
- Building access: customers should ensure access permissions (security gate, parking, lift booking if required).
- **Bulky items:** may require two-person handling; ensure a clear pathway and measure doorways.

## Damaged or missing items
- If your package arrives damaged, report it within **24 hours** with photos of:
  1) outer box
  2) inner packaging
  3) product damage
- Missing item claims should be reported within **48 hours** of delivery confirmation.

## Packaging & sustainability
Where possible, we use minimal packaging. Some manufacturer boxes ship “as-is” (no extra outer box).
