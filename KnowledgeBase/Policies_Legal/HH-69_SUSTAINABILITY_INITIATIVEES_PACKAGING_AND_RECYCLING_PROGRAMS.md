## Packaging Reduction Goals
- Minimize packaging materials where feasible.
- Encourage customers to reuse packaging for returns or storage when safe.

## E-Waste & Battery Recycling
- Dubai Al Quoz store hosts an e-recycling drop-off for small electronics and batteries.
- Provide customers with clear instructions on how to prepare items for recycling.

## Take-Back & Refurbish
- Explore a voluntary take-back program for select models, with refurbishment options where feasible.
- Communicate eligibility and procedures clearly to customers.

## Supplier Requirements
- Encourage suppliers to use recyclable packaging.
- Require labeling that includes recycling instructions.

## Compliance & Reporting
- Track packaging-related waste reductions annually.
- Report improvements in sustainability to management and publish the results in the quarterly ESG digest.
