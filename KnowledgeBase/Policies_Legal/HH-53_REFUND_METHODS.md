# Refund Methods and Timing (HH-53)

## 1. Overview
This policy outlines the standard operating procedures for issuing refunds following a successful product inspection. Timelines and methods are dictated by the original payment channel used during the transaction at **HomeHaven**.

## 2. Refund Scenarios by Payment Method

### Credit & Debit Card Payments (Online/In-Store)
*   **Method:** Reversal to the original card used. 
*   **Timing:** Typically **5–12 business days** after the return has been inspected and approved at our Al Quoz hub.
*   **Note:** The exact posting date depends on your issuing bank’s internal processing cycles.

### Cash on Delivery (COD)
As physical cash cannot be "reversed" via courier, customers may select one of the following:
*   **Bank Transfer:** Funds are wired to a UAE-based IBAN provided by the customer. 
    *   **Timing:** 7–10 business days.
*   **HomeHaven Store Credit:** A digital voucher code sent via SMS/Email. 
    *   **Timing:** Instant (24 hours maximum).

### Buy Now, Pay Later (Tabby / Tamara)
*   **Method:** Adjustment of the remaining installment plan.
*   **Scenario A (Partial Return):** Your remaining installments will be reduced or cancelled. 
*   **Scenario B (Full Return):** Any paid installments are refunded to your card by the provider, and the plan is closed.
*   **Timing:** 3–5 business days for the provider to update their ledger.

## 3. Inspection & Approval Phase
Before a refund is triggered, all items undergo a **Quality Control (QC) Check**:
1.  **Arrival:** Item arrives at the Service Center.
2.  **Verification:** Technicians confirm the item is unused, in original packaging, and matches the serial number on the invoice.
3.  **Authorization:** Once "QC Passed," the Finance Team initiates the payout within 24 hours.

## 4. Important Limitations
*   **Shipping & COD Fees:** Original delivery charges and COD service fees (AED 15) are **non-refundable** unless the return is due to a HomeHaven error or a manufacturing defect.
*   **Bank Holidays:** Timelines exclude UAE Public Holidays and weekends (Saturday/Sunday).
*   **International Cards:** Refunds to non-UAE issued cards may take up to 21 business days due to intermediary bank clearing.
