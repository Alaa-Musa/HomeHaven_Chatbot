# Digital Communications Policy (HH-110)

## 1. Overview
This policy governs the use of digital channels to communicate with customers, ensuring all messaging is purposeful, compliant with anti-spam legislation (e.g., CAN-SPAM, CASL), and respectful of user preferences.

## 2. Channel Utilization & Standards

### Email
*   **Transactional:** Includes order confirmations, shipping updates, and password resets. These are mandatory for service fulfillment and do not require marketing consent.
*   **Marketing:** Includes newsletters, promotions, and personalized recommendations. These require explicit prior consent.
*   **Standards:** All emails must feature a clear "From" line, a physical corporate address, and a functional unsubscribe link.

### SMS (Short Message Service)
*   **Use Case:** Reserved for time-sensitive alerts, such as delivery arrivals or two-factor authentication (2FA) codes.
*   **Frequency:** Marketing SMS is capped at three messages per week to prevent "notification fatigue."
*   **Quiet Hours:** No marketing SMS shall be deployed between 9:00 PM and 8:00 AM in the recipient's local time zone.

### In-App & Push Notifications
*   **In-App:** Used for contextual guidance and feature announcements while the user is active in the application.
*   **Push:** Used for re-engagement and urgent order status changes. Must be used sparingly to avoid OS-level silencing by the user.

## 3. Consent Management & Opt-Outs

### Opt-In Procedures
*   **Explicit Consent:** Marketing enrollment requires a proactive action (e.g., checking an un-ticked box). Pre-ticked boxes are prohibited.
*   **Double Opt-In:** Recommended for SMS to verify the mobile number ownership and intent.

### Opt-Out Instructions
*   **Email:** One-click unsubscribe functionality required in the footer of every marketing transmission.
*   **SMS:** Customers must be able to opt-out by replying "STOP" to any mobile message.
*   **In-App:** Users must have access to a "Notification Settings" menu within the app to toggle specific alert categories.

## 4. Data Retention & Privacy
*   **Consent Logs:** Digital records of when and how a customer opted in must be retained for the duration of the active subscription plus 3 years.
*   **Unsubscribe Propagation:** Opt-out requests must be synchronized across all platforms within **24 hours**.
*   **Retention:** Communication metadata is retained for 13 months for performance analysis, after which it is anonymized or deleted.
