# Affiliate Program Policies (HH-85)

## 1. Overview
The HomeHaven Affiliate Program allows partners to earn commissions by promoting our curated range of kitchen, cleaning, and smart-home essentials. This policy ensures that all referral activities align with our premium brand standards and UAE consumer protection regulations.

## 2. Eligibility & Application
*   **Target Partners:** Content creators, home interior influencers, real estate agencies, and lifestyle platforms with a verified audience in the GCC region.
*   **Verification:** Applicants must provide active social media handles or website URLs. HomeHaven reviews all applications within **5 business days** to ensure content quality and brand alignment.
*   **Account Limits:** One affiliate account per legal entity or individual. Sub-affiliate networks are currently not permitted without prior written consent.

## 3. Commission Structure & Attribution
*   **Standard Rates:** 
    *   **Kitchen & Cleaning:** 5% per successful sale.
    *   **Smart-Home & Electronics:** 3% per successful sale.
    *   **Consumables (Filters/Detergents):** 8% per successful sale.
*   **Cookie Duration:** 30-day tracking window. If a customer clicks your link and purchases within 30 days, the commission is attributed to your account.
*   **Attribution Model:** "Last Click" basis. The final affiliate link clicked before the transaction receives the full commission.
*   **Exclusions:** Commissions are not earned on delivery fees, installation charges, or purchases made using **HavenPlus** points as the sole payment method.

## 4. Compliance & Brand Guidelines
To maintain the integrity of the HomeHaven brand, affiliates must adhere to the following:
*   **Transparency:** Clearly disclose the affiliate relationship to your audience (e.g., using #Ad or #Affiliate) as per UAE National Media Council (NMC) guidelines.
*   **Asset Usage:** Only use high-resolution imagery and logos provided in the **Affiliate Portal**. Altering brand colors or using "low-quality" screenshots is prohibited.
*   **Prohibited Content:** Affiliates may not promote HomeHaven alongside offensive, political, or non-compliant content. 
*   **Search Engine Marketing (SEM):** Bidding on "HomeHaven" or specific product keywords (e.g., "CrispWave price") in Google Ads is strictly forbidden and results in immediate termination.

## 5. Payouts & Reporting
*   **Affiliate Portal:** Real-time tracking of clicks, conversions, and pending commissions is available 24/7.
*   **Payment Threshold:** Minimum payout is **AED 200**.
*   **Schedule:** Payments are processed via bank transfer on the **15th of each month** for the previous month’s cleared earnings.
*   **Returns Impact:** Commissions for returned items (per **HH-52**) will be clawed back or deducted from the next payout cycle.

## 6. Termination
HomeHaven reserves the right to suspend any affiliate found engaging in "Coupon Stuffing," cookie-dropping, or misrepresenting product specifications (e.g., claiming a **DustDash** has a 2-hour runtime).
