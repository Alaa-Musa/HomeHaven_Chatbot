# Delivery Pricing and Cut-off Times (HH-28)

## 1. Overview
To maintain a reliable supply chain for HomeHaven household essentials, we operate a tiered delivery system across the UAE. Logistics are optimized from our central Al Quoz hub to ensure that high-demand items—like the **CrispWave** or **AirPure** series—reach urban centers with maximum efficiency.

## 2. Delivery Service Matrix


| Option | Availability | Price | Order Cut-off | Typical Window |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard** | All Emirates | **Free** on AED 199+ <br> (AED 15 below) | 6:00 PM | **Next-day** (Major Cities) <br> 2–3 days (Remote Areas) |
| **Express** | Dubai & Abu Dhabi | **AED 35** | 12:00 PM | **Same-day** (16:00 – 22:00) |

## 3. Service Definitions & Restrictions

### Standard Delivery
*   **Major Cities:** Includes Dubai, Abu Dhabi City, Sharjah City, and Ajman.
*   **Other Areas:** Includes Al Ain, Western Region (Ghayathi/Liwa), and Northern Emirates (Fujairah/RAK/UAQ).
*   **Threshold:** The AED 199 free-shipping threshold is calculated on the net cart value after all HavenPlus discounts are applied.

### Express Delivery (Same-Day)
*   **Urgency:** Designed for immediate household needs (e.g., a broken blender or urgent air purification).
*   **Geographic Limit:** Only available within the municipal limits of Dubai and Abu Dhabi. Not available for "Bulky/Installed" items (HH-54).
*   **Real-Time Tracking:** Customers receive a live GPS link via SMS once the driver leaves the hub.

### Click & Collect (In-Store Pickup)
*   **Verification:** You must present your **Order QR Code** and a valid Emirates ID or Passport.
*   **Proxy Pickup:** If sending someone else, use the **Authorization Form (HH-65B)** via the app.
*   **Unclaimed Orders:** Items not collected within 72 hours are returned to inventory, and a refund is initiated (minus a 5% restocking fee).

## 4. Operational Notes
*   **Public Holidays:** Delivery windows may be extended during UAE National Day, Eid Al Fitr, and Eid Al Adha.
*   **Bulky Items:** Scheduled delivery for large appliances (HH-54) follows a separate 4-hour slot system and does not qualify for Express 12:00 PM cut-offs.
*   **Inclement Weather:** During rare heavy rain or sandstorms, Express windows may be downgraded to Standard for driver safety.
