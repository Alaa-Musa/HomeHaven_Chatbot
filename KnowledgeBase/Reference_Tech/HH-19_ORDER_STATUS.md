## Status glossary
- **Confirmed:** payment authorized (or COD confirmed) and order placed
- **Processing:** items being picked/packed
- **Shipped:** handed to courier
- **Out for delivery:** courier is delivering today
- **Delivered:** courier marked delivered
- **Ready for pickup:** Click & Collect items prepared
- **Picked up:** collected from store
- **Cancelled:** order cancelled before fulfillment
- **Return requested:** customer initiated return
- **Returned:** items received back and under inspection
- **Refunded:** refund issued (timing depends on bank/payment method)
