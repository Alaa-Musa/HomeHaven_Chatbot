# Warranty Exclusions — Quick Reference (HH-25)

## 1. Overview
While **HomeHaven** products are engineered for the rigors of daily UAE household life, the standard warranty is specifically designed to protect against manufacturing defects and component failures. This reference outlines scenarios and items that fall outside of that protection.

## 2. Standard Exclusions (Not Covered)

### Physical & Accidental Damage
*   **Impact:** Cracks, dents, or shattered glass (e.g., a dropped **BB-CM125** carafe) resulting from accidental drops or mishandling.
*   **Liquid Ingress:** Damage caused by internal spills or submersion, unless the product carries a specific IP-rating (e.g., "Waterproof") and was used within those parameters.

### Consumables & High-Wear Parts
As defined in **HH-51**, these items naturally degrade and are the owner's responsibility to replace:
*   **Filters:** Clogged HEPA or carbon filters.
*   **Mechanical Wear:** Thinning bristles on vacuum brush rolls or dulled blender blades.
*   **Battery Capacity:** Normal chemical aging of lithium-ion cells (e.g., shorter runtimes after 6+ months of use).

### Infrastructure & External Factors
*   **Voltage Issues:** Damage caused by power surges or the use of incorrect voltage converters/travel adapters.
*   **Improper Installation:** Failure resulting from DIY setup that contradicts the **HH-62** guidelines (e.g., reversed wiring on a **CC-T1**).
*   **Environmental Stress:** Corrosive damage or overheating caused by storing indoor appliances in high-humidity or high-heat outdoor areas (e.g., balconies or non-climate-controlled garages).

### Unauthorized Intervention
*   **Third-Party Repair:** Any attempt to open the chassis or repair the unit by a non-certified technician.
*   **Modifications:** Use of non-genuine HomeHaven parts (e.g., third-party filters) that leads to motor or system failure.

## 3. Determining Coverage
If you are unsure whether a fault is a "Manufacturing Defect" or "Accidental Damage":
*   **Step 1:** Consult the **Warranty Claim Checklist (HH-24)**.
*   **Step 2:** Submit high-resolution photos and a video of the fault to our technical team.
*   **Step 3:** Our specialists will perform a diagnostic to determine if the failure was caused by a component flaw (covered) or an external stressor (not covered).

## 4. Post-Warranty Options
If your item is excluded from warranty coverage:
*   **Paid Repair:** HomeHaven offers out-of-warranty repair services at our Al Quoz hub (subject to parts and labor fees).
*   **Trade-In:** You may be eligible for a **HavenPlus** discount toward a replacement unit through our "Green-Cycle" recycling program.
