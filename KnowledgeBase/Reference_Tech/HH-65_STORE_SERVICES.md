# In-Store Services Overview (HH-65)

## 1. Overview
While **HomeHaven** is a digital-first retailer, our physical Experience Centers across the UAE serve as critical hubs for logistics, sustainability, and face-to-face customer support. Each location is equipped to handle omnichannel fulfillment and post-purchase service.

## 2. Core In-Store Services

### Click & Collect (Ready in 2 Hours)
*   **Availability:** Offered at all three flagship locations.
*   **Process:** Select "Store Pickup" at checkout. You will receive an SMS/Email with a unique QR code once your items are staged and ready.
*   **Holding Period:** Orders are held for **3 days**. After this period, the items are returned to stock and a refund is issued to your original payment method.

### In-Store Returns & Exchanges
*   **Eligibility:** Items purchased online or in-store can be returned within 30 days, provided they meet the "Shelf-Ready" criteria outlined in **HH-52**.
*   **Immediate Inspection:** Our on-site technicians can verify the condition of appliances (e.g., the PB-900 or BB-CM125) immediately, speeding up the refund authorization.
*   **Exchanges:** Direct swaps for different colors or models are available subject to local stock availability.

### Specialized E-Recycling (Sustainability Initiative)
*   **Location:** **Dubai — Al Quoz Store Only.**
*   **Service:** A dedicated E-Recycling Drop Box is available for small-to-medium household electronics (e.g., old blenders, thermostats, or air purifier motors).
*   **Incentive:** HavenPlus members receive **100 "Green Points"** per recycled small appliance (maximum 500 points per month).

## 4. In-Store Safety & Access
*   **Parking:** Dedicated customer parking is available at all locations. The Al Quoz hub includes a **Loading Zone** for large appliance pickups.
*   **Assistance:** Store staff are trained to demonstrate the **HomeHaven App** and assist with "Smart Home" pairing for the CC-T1 and SN-L2 series.
