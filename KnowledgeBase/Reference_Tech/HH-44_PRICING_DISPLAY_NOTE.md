# Pricing Display and VAT Note (HH-44)

## 1. Overview
At **HomeHaven**, we prioritize pricing transparency to ensure a seamless checkout experience for our UAE customers. All financial figures across our digital platforms and retail Experience Centers are presented in the local currency with mandatory tax inclusions as per federal regulations.

## 2. Currency and Display
*   **Local Currency:** All prices are displayed and transacted in **United Arab Emirates Dirhams (AED)**. 
*   **Exchange Rates:** For customers using international cards, the final charged amount may fluctuate based on your issuing bank’s daily exchange rate and foreign transaction fees.

## 3. Value Added Tax (VAT)
In accordance with UAE Federal Tax Authority (FTA) regulations:
*   **Inclusive Pricing:** Product pages and shelf labels show **VAT-inclusive prices** (currently 5%) unless explicitly stated otherwise. The price you see on the product detail sheet (e.g., for the **PureBlend PB-900**) is the final price for the item.
*   **Tax Invoices:** A simplified or full Tax Invoice—detailing the specific VAT amount, the HomeHaven TRN (Tax Registration Number), and the subtotal—is issued for every transaction.
*   **Digital Access:** Your Tax Invoice is sent via email immediately upon order confirmation and remains accessible in the "Order History" section of the **HomeHaven App**.

## 4. Breakdown of Charges
While the product price includes VAT, the following may be listed as separate line items during the final checkout phase:
*   **Standard/Express Shipping Fees:** Subject to 5% VAT (included in the quoted shipping price).
*   **Installation Services:** Professional assembly or setup fees (e.g., **AED 149 for dishwashers**) include VAT.
*   **COD Convenience Fees:** The **AED 15** service charge for Cash on Delivery is a service fee and includes applicable VAT.

## 5. Commercial and Tax-Exempt Entities
*   **Business Purchases:** Corporate entities requiring a formal Tax Invoice with their own TRN must provide these details in the "Company Information" section at checkout. 
*   **Tax Refunds:** HomeHaven does not currently participate in the "Tourist Tax Refund" scheme for in-store purchases. All sales are final regarding tax collection at the point of purchase.
