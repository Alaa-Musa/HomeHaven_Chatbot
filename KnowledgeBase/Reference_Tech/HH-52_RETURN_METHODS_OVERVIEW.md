# Returns — Methods Overview: Courier vs. In-Store (HH-52)

## 1. Overview
HomeHaven provides flexible return options for our UAE customers. Whether you prefer the convenience of a home pickup or the immediate resolution of an in-store visit, our goal is a seamless reversal of your purchase within our **30-day eligibility window**.

## 2. Return Methods

### Courier Pickup (Home/Office)
*   **Availability:** Serviceable across all seven Emirates, including major residential hubs and remote areas.
*   **Booking:** Initiated via the "My Orders" section on the HomeHaven app or website.
*   **Fees:** 
    *   **Defective/Wrong Item:** Pickup is **Free of Charge**.
    *   **Change-of-Mind:** A flat-rate courier fee (typically AED 25–50 depending on item size) will be deducted from your final refund.
*   **Process:** Our logistics partner will contact you within 24–48 hours to coordinate a 4-hour pickup window.

### In-Store Returns (Drop-off)
Visit any of our flagship Experience Centers for an immediate inspection and refund processing.
*   **Dubai:** Al Quoz Industrial Area 3 (Main Warehouse & Showroom).
*   **Abu Dhabi:** Al Khalidiya, Zayed the First St.
*   **Sharjah:** Al Majaz 2, Corniche St.
*   **Benefit:** No shipping fees apply to in-store returns, regardless of the reason for return.

## 3. Eligibility & Condition Requirements
To ensure a successful return, all items must meet the following "Shelf-Ready" criteria:
*   **Original Packaging:** Items must be returned in their original, undamaged branded box with all internal protective styrofoam/cardboard.
*   **Unused Status:** Products must show no signs of assembly, usage, or wear. For hygiene reasons, opened cleaning solutions or used vacuum filters cannot be returned.
*   **Documentation:** A digital or physical copy of the **Tax Invoice** must be presented.
*   **Inclusions:** All accessories, manuals, and promotional "free gifts" must be included in the return package.

## 4. Refund Timelines
*   **Credit/Debit Card:** 7–14 business days (depending on your bank’s processing time).
*   **Tabby/Tamara:** Installment adjustments are usually reflected within 5 business days.
*   **Store Credit:** Issued immediately upon successful inspection (valid for 12 months).
