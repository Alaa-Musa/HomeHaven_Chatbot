## Who we are
**HomeHaven Household** is a UAE household retailer offering practical products for daily home living—kitchen appliances, cleaning solutions, and smart-home essentials—through our website, mobile app, and selected stores.

Customers can reach us through the following email addresses:

support@homehaven.ae
urgent@homehaven.ae
claims@homehaven.ae
privacy@homehaven.ae

## Our physical stores

While **HomeHaven Household** primarily serves our customers online, we also invite you to visit us in person at our three UAE locations:
- Dubai (Main Branch): Ibn Battuta Mall, Sheikh Zayed Road, Jebel Ali.
- Abu Dhabi: Al Forsan Mall, Abdulrahman Bin Awf St, Khalifa City.
- Sharjah: Mega Mall, Al Istiqlal Street, Al Bu Daniq.

## What we sell
- Kitchen appliances: air fryers, blenders, kettles, coffee machines
- Cleaning & floor care: cordless vacuums, steam mops, detergents
- Smart home: smart locks, thermostats, sensors, smart plugs
- Selected built-in appliances: dishwashers and water filtration kits (limited models)

## How we deliver
- Standard delivery across the UAE
- Express delivery (selected cities)
- Free Click & Collect at participating stores

## Support hours
Customer support is available **daily 9:00–21:00 (GST)** via chat, email, and phone.

## Sustainability note (store-style statement)
We offer e-recycling drop-off boxes at the Dubai (Al Quoz) store for small electronics and batteries.
