# Address Requirement — No P.O. Boxes (HH-56)

## 1. Overview
To ensure the successful hand-to-hand delivery of household goods and appliances, **HomeHaven** requires a precise physical location. This policy outlines our addressing standards to prevent shipping delays or lost consignments within the UAE.

## 2. Addressing Standards

### Physical Address Requirement
*   **No P.O. Boxes:** We do **not** deliver to P.O. Box addresses. Our courier partners require a physical street address to complete the "last-mile" delivery and obtain a proof-of-delivery (POD) signature.
*   **Building/Villa Details:** Customers must provide a complete address including:
    *   Villa/Apartment Number
    *   Building Name / Street Name
    *   Area/District (e.g., Jumeirah 1, Khalifa City, Al Majaz)
    *   City (Emirate)

### Contact Information
*   **Mandatory Phone Number:** A valid UAE mobile number (+971) is required for every order. Couriers will call or WhatsApp prior to arrival to confirm the sub-location or gate access.
*   **Alternative Contact:** For gated communities or high-security buildings, providing a secondary number (e.g., spouse or household staff) is highly recommended.

## 3. Why Physical Addresses are Required
*   **Signature Verification:** Most HomeHaven items (especially high-value electronics) require a recipient signature upon delivery.
*   **Bulky Item Handling:** Large appliances require specific entry points (service elevators/loading docks) that cannot be serviced via a central post office box.
*   **Real-Time Tracking:** Our GPS-linked tracking system relies on coordinates to provide "Stops Away" updates to the customer.

## 4. Incorrect Address Protocol
*   **Correction Window:** If you realize a P.O. Box or incorrect address was provided, contact **Customer Care** within **2 hours** of placing the order.
*   **Return to Hub:** Shipments addressed solely to a P.O. Box will be flagged by our sorting facility and held at the Al Quoz hub until a physical address is verified.
*   **Storage Fees:** If a physical address is not provided within **48 hours** of the hold, the order may be cancelled, and a restocking fee may apply.
