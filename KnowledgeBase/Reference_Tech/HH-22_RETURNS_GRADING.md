## Grades
### Grade A (Resellable)
Unused, complete, original packaging intact.

### Grade B (Open-box)
Packaging opened but product appears unused; minor packaging wear.

### Grade C (Incomplete)
Missing accessories/manuals/free gifts OR heavy packaging damage.

### Grade D (Rejected)
Used signs, mismatched serial, damage from misuse/liquid ingress, or unauthorized repair.

## Outcomes
- Grade A/B: eligible for refund per policy
- Grade C: partial refund may be offered at support discretion
- Grade D: refund refused; item may be returned to customer
