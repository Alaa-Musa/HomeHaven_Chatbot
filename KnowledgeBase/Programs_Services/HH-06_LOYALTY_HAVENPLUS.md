## Earning points
- Earn **1 point per AED 1** spent on eligible items.
- Points are awarded after the order is marked “delivered” or “picked up”.

### Exclusions
Points are not earned on:
- Delivery fees
- Gift cards
- Installation fees
- Extended warranty add-ons (if offered)

## Redeeming points
- **500 points = AED 25** discount at checkout.
- You can redeem in increments of 500 points.
- Points cannot be exchanged for cash.

## Expiry
- Points expire **12 months after earning** (rolling expiry).
Example: points earned on 2026-02-01 expire on 2027-02-01.

## Returns and points
- If you return an item, the points earned on that item are deducted.
- If you used points on an order and then return items, the refund is adjusted accordingly.

## Membership tiers (simple)
- **Classic:** default for all members
- **Plus:** earned after spending AED 2,000 in a rolling 12-month period
  - Benefits: early access to promos + dedicated chat queue (when available)

## Account notes
Only one HavenPlus account per phone number. HomeHaven may suspend accounts for suspected abuse.
