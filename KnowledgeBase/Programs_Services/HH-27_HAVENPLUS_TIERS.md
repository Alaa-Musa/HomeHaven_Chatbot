# HavenPlus — Tier Rules and Benefits (HH-27)

## 1. Overview
The **HavenPlus** loyalty program is structured to reward our most frequent HomeHaven shoppers. By reaching specific spending milestones within the UAE market, members unlock enhanced service levels and exclusive access to our premium household catalog.

## 2. Membership Tiers

### HavenPlus Classic
*   **Status:** Default entry-level tier for all registered users.
*   **Requirement:** Successful account creation via the HomeHaven App or Website.
*   **Core Benefit:** Standard earning rate of 1 point for every AED 1 spent on qualifying products.

### HavenPlus Premium (Plus)
*   **Status:** Elevated tier for frequent shoppers and smart-home early adopters.
*   **Requirement:** Total qualifying spend of **AED 2,000** within a rolling 12-month period.
*   **Maintenance:** To maintain "Plus" status, the AED 2,000 spending threshold must be met annually.

## 3. Plus Tier Exclusive Benefits
Once a member reaches the Plus threshold, the following enhancements are activated:
*   **Early Access:** 24-hour advance window to shop Seasonal Sales (e.g., Ramadan Specials, White Friday) before they are released to the general public.
*   **Dedicated Chat Queue:** Priority routing in the HomeHaven App's "Live Support" module, reducing wait times during peak hours.
*   **Exclusive Beta Testing:** Opportunity to purchase "First-Run" units of new Series L-9 or CrispWave releases before the official UAE launch.
*   **Birthday Reward:** A one-time 500-point bonus (AED 25 value) credited during your birthday month.

## 4. Program Integrity & Rules
*   **Identity Sync:** Only **one account per unique UAE mobile number** (+971) is permitted. Points cannot be merged across multiple accounts.
*   **Non-Transferable:** Benefits and tier status are tied to the individual account holder and cannot be gifted or sold.
*   **Abuse Policy:** HomeHaven reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts suspected of loyalty fraud, including:
    *   Creating multiple accounts to bypass redemption limits.
    *   Bulk-buying for commercial resale using personal loyalty benefits.
    *   Exploiting return loops to artificially inflate point balances.

## 5. Tier Tracking
Members can track their progress toward the "Plus" tier via the **Progress Bar** in the HavenPlus dashboard. This displays the remaining spend required (in AED) to unlock the next level of benefits.
