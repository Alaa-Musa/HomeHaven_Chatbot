# HavenPlus — Points Examples: Worked Scenarios (HH-26)

## 1. Overview
To help members maximize their savings, this guide provides practical mathematical examples of how **HavenPlus** points are earned, redeemed, and managed throughout the customer lifecycle at HomeHaven.

## 2. Earning Scenarios (Standard 1:1 Ratio)
Points are calculated based on the final AED value of the product (excluding VAT, shipping, and service fees).

*   **Scenario A: Kitchen Essentials**
    *   Purchase: **PureBlend Blender (PB-900)** at AED 249.
    *   Action: Order is delivered to your villa in Khalifa City.
    *   **Result:** **249 points** credited to your account within 24 hours.
*   **Scenario B: Smart Home Upgrade**
    *   Purchase: **SecureNest Smart Lock (SN-L2)** at AED 549.
    *   Action: Installation is completed by a HomeHaven technician (HH-62).
    *   **Result:** **549 points** credited (Note: The AED 99 installation fee does not earn points).

## 3. Redeeming Scenarios (The 500-Point Rule)
Points are redeemed in fixed blocks of 500. Each block represents a **5% cash-back value** (AED 25).

*   **Level 1 Redemption:**
    *   Balance: 650 points.
    *   Redeem: **500 points**.
    *   **Benefit:** **AED 25 discount** applied to your current cart. (150 points remain).
*   **Level 2 Redemption:**
    *   Balance: 1,200 points.
    *   Redeem: **1,000 points**.
    *   **Benefit:** **AED 50 discount** applied to your current cart. (200 points remain).

## 4. Rolling Expiry Logic
HomeHaven utilizes a "First-In, First-Out" (FIFO) expiry system to give you a full year to use every point earned.

*   **Date Earned:** February 1, 2026 (e.g., from a **CC-T1 Thermostat** purchase).
*   **Validity:** 365 Days.
*   **Expiry Date:** **February 1, 2027**.
*   *Tip: Check the "Points Activity" tab in the HomeHaven App to see a 30-day warning for any upcoming expiring blocks.*

## 5. Returns & Refunds Impact
When a transaction is reversed, the associated points must also be reconciled.

*   **Scenario: Partial Return**
    1.  You buy two **AirPure AP-400** units (Earned: 800 points).
    2.  You return one unit for a refund.
    3.  **Result:** **400 points** are automatically deducted from your balance.
*   **Scenario: Points-Used Return**
    1.  You use 500 points (AED 25 value) to buy a **BB-CM125 Coffee Maker**.
    2.  You return the coffee maker.
    3.  **Result:** You receive a cash/card refund for the amount paid, and the **500 points** are restored to your HavenPlus account.
