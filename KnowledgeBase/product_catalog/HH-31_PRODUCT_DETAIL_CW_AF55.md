# Product Detail Sheet — CrispWave Air Fryer CW-AF55 (HH-31)

## 1. Overview
The **CrispWave CW-AF55** is a 5.5L high-capacity air fryer designed for healthy UAE households. Utilizing 360° Rapid Air Circulation, it achieves a crispy "deep-fried" texture using up to 85% less oil than traditional frying methods, making it ideal for family-sized portions of local favorites.

## 2. Key Specifications
*   **Total Capacity:** 5.5 Liters (fits a whole 1.2kg chicken or 800g of fries).
*   **Temperature Range:** Precision control from **80°C to 200°C**.
*   **Timer:** 0–60 minute programmable timer with an audible ready-signal.
*   **Power:** 1700W High-Efficiency heating element.
*   **Digital Interface:** LED Touch Control Panel with real-time temperature display.

## 3. Intelligent Presets & Modes
The CW-AF55 includes five "One-Touch" smart programs calibrated for optimal results:
*   **Fries:** 200°C / 18 mins (Recommended: Shake at 9 mins).
*   **Chicken:** 190°C / 25 mins (Ideal for wings or drumsticks).
*   **Veg:** 160°C / 10 mins (Perfect for roasted cauliflower or broccoli).
*   **Reheat:** 120°C / 5 mins (Restores crispness to leftover manakish or samosas).
*   **Bake:** 160°C / 30 mins (Suitable for small cakes or muffins).

## 4. Safety & Ergonomics
*   **Auto Shut-Off:** The unit automatically pauses heating when the basket is removed and shuts off completely when the timer hits zero.
*   **Cool-Touch Handle:** Advanced thermal insulation prevents heat transfer to the grip during high-temperature cycles.
*   **Overheat Protection:** Integrated thermal fuse prevents internal component damage during back-to-back cooking sessions.
*   **Non-Slip Feet:** High-friction rubber base prevents "walking" on granite or tiled kitchen counters.

## 5. Care and Maintenance
*   **Non-Stick Preservation:** The basket features a premium PFOA-free coating. **Hand washing with a soft sponge is strongly recommended** to extend the life of the non-stick surface.
*   **Dishwasher Safety:** While the basket is technically top-rack dishwasher safe, harsh detergents and high-heat drying cycles may cause the coating to flake over time.
*   **Exterior:** Wipe the matte-finish housing with a damp microfiber cloth only. Never submerge the main unit in water.

## 6. Warranty & Service
*   **Standard Term:** 1-year manufacturer warranty covering electrical and motor defects.
*   **Exclusions:** Damage to the non-stick coating due to metal utensils or abrasive cleaning is not covered (refer to **HH-25**).
*   **Support:** For troubleshooting or spare parts (e.g., replacement rubber feet), contact **Customer Support (HH-30)**.
