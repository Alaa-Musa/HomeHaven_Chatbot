## Overview
The **Veridian Clean Series** represents our fictional premium line of high-efficiency (HE) laundry and specialized surface cleaners. These expanded detail sheets provide technical specifications for professional-grade results in residential settings.

## Product Specifications (L-9 Detergent)
- Formula Type: Ultra-concentrated Bio-Enzymatic Liquid.

- Active Ingredients:

-- 1. Protease & Amylase Enzymes: For breaking down protein and starch-based stains.

-- 2. Non-ionic Surfactants (AEO-9): For superior oil and grease emulsification.

- pH Level: 7.5 – 8.5 (Neutral-balanced to protect delicate fibers).

- Viscosity: 300–500 cPs for optimal dispensing in automatic tray systems.

- Concentration: 4x Strength (use as directed, for example in a 15ml per standard 7kg load).

## Compatibility & Performance

Machine Compatibility: Fully compatible with High-Efficiency (HE) front-loaders and standard top-loading machines.
Water Temperature:
Optimal: 15°C to 40°C (Cold to Warm).
Max Temp: 60°C for heavy-duty sanitation cycles.
Fabric Safety:
Recommended: Cotton, Synthetics, Linens, and Blends.
Not Recommended: Natural Silk or Unfinished Leather (Requires L-9 Delicate Variant).
Mixing Guidelines:
Safe: May be used alongside Oxygen-based bleaches or fabric softeners if added to separate dispensers.
DANGER: Never mix with Ammonia or Chlorine-based products as this may produce toxic gases.

## Care and Storage

Storage: Store in a cool, dry environment between 5°C and 30°C. Keep in original container with the label visible.
Shelf Life: 24 months from the date of manufacture when sealed.
Handling: Keep out of reach of children. If contact with eyes occurs, rinse immediately with water for 15 minutes.
