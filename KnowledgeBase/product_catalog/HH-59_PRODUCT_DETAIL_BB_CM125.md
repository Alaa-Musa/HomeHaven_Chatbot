# Product Detail Sheet — BrewBuddy Coffee Maker BB-CM125 (HH-59)

## 1. Overview
The **BrewBuddy BB-CM125** is a classic drip-filtration coffee system designed for high-capacity household brewing. Engineered for reliability and ease of use, it delivers consistent extraction for up to 10–12 cups of coffee per cycle.

## 2. Technical Specifications & Highlights
*   **Brewing Capacity:** **1.25L** High-Borosilicate Glass Carafe (Impact and heat resistant).
*   **Keep Warm Function:** Integrated non-stick warming plate maintains optimal serving temperature (75°C–80°C) for up to **40 minutes** before automatic safety shut-off.
*   **Extraction System:** Multi-hole showerhead ensures even saturation of coffee grounds for a balanced flavor profile.
*   **Anti-Drip Valve:** "Pause-and-Serve" feature allows for pouring a cup mid-brew by temporarily sealing the filter basket for up to 30 seconds.

## 3. Included Accessories
*   **Washable Permanent Filter:** High-density nylon mesh filter included. Eliminates the need for disposable paper filters (though Size 4 paper filters may be used for a cleaner, sediment-free cup).
*   **Measuring Scoop:** Standardized 7g scoop for consistent dosing.
*   **Removable Filter Basket:** Easy-lift handle for quick disposal of used grounds and thorough cleaning.

## 4. Operational Guidelines
*   **Coffee Type:** **Works with medium-grind ground coffee only.** This unit is not compatible with capsules, pods, or whole beans (requires a separate grinder).
*   **Water Level:** Use the transparent water window to monitor levels. Do not exceed the "MAX" line to prevent overflow during the boiling phase.
*   **Initial Setup:** Before first use, run two full brewing cycles with plain water (no coffee) to sterilize the internal plumbing.

## 5. Care & Maintenance
*   **Descaling:** In regions with hard water (common in some UAE districts), descale the unit every 60 uses using a 50/50 white vinegar and water solution to prevent calcium buildup on the heating element.
*   **Cleaning:**
    *   **Carafe & Filter:** Dishwasher safe (top rack only), though hand washing is recommended to prevent glass etching.
    *   **Housing:** Wipe the exterior with a damp cloth only. Never submerge the base unit in water.
*   **Safety:** Always unplug the unit when the water reservoir is empty or when the device is not in use for extended periods.
