# Product Detail Sheet — SteamShine Steam Mop SS-M10 (HH-34)

## 1. Overview
The **SteamShine SS-M10** is a high-temperature steam cleaning system engineered for deep sanitization of tiled and sealed floor surfaces. By converting plain water into pressurized steam, it eliminates 99.9% of common household bacteria and grime without the use of harsh chemicals, making it ideal for UAE homes with children and pets.

## 2. Technical Specifications & Highlights
*   **Heat-Up Time:** Rapid-start boiler technology; **ready in 25 seconds**.
*   **Water Tank Capacity:** 350ml (provides approx. 12–15 minutes of continuous steam).
*   **Steam Temperature:** 110°C at the nozzle for effective thermal sanitization.
*   **Power Cord:** 5-meter heavy-duty cable for extended reach in large living areas.
*   **Swivel Head:** 180° maneuverability for cleaning around furniture legs and into tight corners.

## 3. Included Accessories
*   **Microfiber Pads (x2):** High-absorbency, triple-layer pads designed to lift and trap loosened dirt.
*   **Carpet Glider:** A clip-on frame that allows the mop to slide over low-pile rugs and carpets to refresh fibers and remove odors.
*   **Measuring Cup:** For precise, spill-free refilling of the internal reservoir.

## 4. Critical Floor Compatibility Warning
The SS-M10 uses high-heat moisture which can damage sensitive surfaces.
*   **Recommended:** Sealed ceramic tiles, porcelain, marble, and sealed laminate.
*   **NOT Recommended:** **Unsealed wood**, **waxed floors**, or highly porous stone. The heat may strip wax finishes or cause unsealed wood planks to warp and swell.
*   **Test Patch:** Always perform a 60-second test on an inconspicuous corner of the floor before proceeding with full-room cleaning.

## 5. Care & Maintenance
*   **Decalcification:** In areas with hard water, use distilled or demineralized water to prevent calcium scale from clogging the internal steam pump.
*   **Pad Care:** Microfiber pads are machine washable (up to 60°C). Do not use fabric softener or bleach, as this reduces the pads' dirt-trapping effectiveness.
*   **Storage:** Empty any remaining water from the tank after every use to prevent mold growth and mineral deposits. Store upright in a cool, dry place.

## 6. Safety Features
*   **Steam-on-Demand:** Trigger-controlled steam release prevents accidental moisture buildup when the unit is stationary.
*   **Thermal Cut-off:** Automatically disables the heating element if the water tank runs dry.
