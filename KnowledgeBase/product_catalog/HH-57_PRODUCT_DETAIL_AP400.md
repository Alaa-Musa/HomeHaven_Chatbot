# Product Detail Sheet — AirPure Air Purifier AP-400 (HH-57)

## 1. Overview
The **AirPure AP-400** is a high-performance atmospheric filtration system designed for UAE households. Engineered for desert environments, it specifically targets fine dust (PM2.5), seasonal pollens, and common household odors through a triple-stage purification process.

## 2. Technical Specifications & Highlights
*   **Effective Coverage Area:** Optimized for rooms up to **35 m²** (approx. 375 sq. ft.). Ideal for master bedrooms, mid-sized living areas, and home offices.
*   **Clean Air Delivery Rate (CADR):** 260 m³/h.
*   **Noise Levels:** 
    *   **Sleep Mode:** <25 dB (Whisper-quiet for night use).
    *   **Turbo/Auto:** Max 52 dB.
*   **Power Consumption:** 45W Energy-Efficient DC Motor.
*   **Sensor Technology:** Integrated Laser Dust Sensor for real-time air quality monitoring (displayed via LED color ring: Blue/Green/Orange/Red).

## 3. Filtration System
The AP-400 utilizes a 3-in-1 integrated drum filter for maximum surface area exposure:
1.  **Fabric Pre-filter:** Captures large particles such as pet hair, lint, and large dust fibers. (Washable/Vacuumable).
2.  **H13 HEPA-Style Filter:** Traps 99.97% of airborne particles as small as 0.3 microns, including smoke and microscopic allergens.
3.  **Activated Carbon Layer:** Neutralizes VOCs (Volatile Organic Compounds), cooking smells, and formaldehydes.

## 4. Operational Modes
*   **Auto Mode:** Automatically adjusts fan speed based on detected particulate matter (PM2.5) levels.
*   **Sleep Mode:** Dims all display lights and locks the fan to the lowest speed setting for undisturbed rest.
*   **Timer Function:** Programmable 2h, 4h, and 8h shut-off intervals.

## 5. Maintenance & Support
### Filter Replacement
*   **Replacement Pack:** **AP-F400** (Genuine HomeHaven multi-layer filter).
*   **Life Expectancy:** 6–8 months depending on air quality and usage. The "Filter Reset" indicator will flash when a change is required.

### Critical Setup Note (New Units)
*   **Plastic Removal:** Upon unboxing, you must open the bottom hatch, remove the filter, and **take off the protective plastic packaging**. Operating the unit with the plastic on will cause motor overheating and zero filtration.

### Troubleshooting
*   **Weak Airflow:** Check if the intake grilles are blocked by curtains/furniture. Ensure the pre-filter is vacuumed (recommended every 2 weeks).
*   **Unusual Noise:** Ensure the unit is on a level surface and no foreign objects have fallen through the top exhaust grille.
*   **Odors Persist:** If the unit emits a "sour" smell, the carbon layer is saturated; replace the filter immediately.
