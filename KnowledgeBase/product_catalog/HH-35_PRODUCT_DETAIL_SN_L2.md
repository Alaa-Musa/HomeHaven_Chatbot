# Product Detail Sheet — SecureNest Smart Lock SN-L2 (HH-35)

## 1. Overview
The **SecureNest SN-L2** is a premium biometric smart lock designed for high-security residential access in the UAE. Engineered for durability and ease of use, it replaces standard deadbolt or mortise systems to provide keyless entry for modern HomeHaven households.

## 2. Technical Specifications & Unlock Methods
The SN-L2 offers four redundant entry methods to ensure you are never locked out:
*   **Biometric Fingerprint:** High-resolution 3D semiconductor sensor (stores up to 50 unique prints). Recognition speed: < 0.5 seconds.
*   **Touchscreen PIN:** Anti-peep keypad allows you to enter random digits before or after your real code to prevent shoulder surfing. (Stores up to 20 codes).
*   **Mobile App Control:** Connect via **Bluetooth** within 10 meters to unlock, manage user permissions, and view entry logs through the HomeHaven App.
*   **Physical Key Backup:** Includes 2 high-security mechanical override keys for emergency use.

## 3. Power & Battery Management
*   **Power Source:** 4x AA Alkaline batteries (included).
*   **Battery Life:** Typical usage lasts **6–9 months**. Battery performance may vary based on door alignment and frequency of motor engagement.
*   **Low Battery Alert:** The unit will emit a series of audible beeps and a red light flash when power drops below 15%.
*   **Emergency Jumpstart:** Features a **Micro-USB/USB-C emergency port** at the bottom of the exterior panel. If batteries die completely, a power bank can be used to provide temporary power for a PIN/Fingerprint entry.

## 4. Hardware & Compatibility
*   **Door Thickness:** Fits standard doors ranging from **35mm to 55mm**.
*   **Material:** Reinforced Zinc Alloy chassis with an electroplated anti-corrosion finish (suitable for indoor and climate-controlled hallway environments).
*   **Mortise Type:** Compatible with most standard 60/72 mortise locks.

## 5. Professional Installation
While the SN-L2 includes a DIY mounting kit, HomeHaven recommends our **Professional Installation Service** to ensure perfect bolt alignment and prevent motor strain.
*   **Installation Fee:** **AED 99** (covers mounting, strike plate adjustment, and app pairing).
*   **Pre-Install Check:** Please refer to the **SN-L2 Installation Guide (HH-62)** to confirm your door material and lock-hole dimensions before booking.

## 6. Safety & Security Features
*   **Auto-Lock:** Automatically engages the deadbolt 5 seconds after the door is closed (can be toggled off in settings).
*   **Lockout Mode:** After 5 incorrect attempts, the keypad freezes for 3 minutes to prevent brute-force attacks.
*   **Privacy Mode:** An internal "Do Not Disturb" switch disables all external electronic access (Fingerprint/PIN) from the inside.
