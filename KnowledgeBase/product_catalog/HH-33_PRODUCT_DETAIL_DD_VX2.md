# Product Detail Sheet — DustDash Cordless Vacuum DD-VX2 (HH-33)

## 1. Overview
The **DustDash DD-VX2** is a high-maneuverability cordless vacuum cleaner specifically balanced for UAE apartments and villas. Featuring a brushless digital motor and a multi-stage filtration system, it effectively captures fine desert dust and pet hair from both hard floors and low-pile carpets.

## 2. Technical Specifications & Highlights
*   **Motor Type:** 250W High-Efficiency Brushless DC (BLDC) Motor.
*   **Battery System:** 2200mAh Removable Lithium-Ion Battery.
*   **Operational Runtime:** 
    *   **Eco Mode:** Up to 35 minutes (Ideal for hard floors and daily dusting).
    *   **Boost Mode:** 12–15 minutes (High-suction for carpets and stubborn debris).
*   **Weight:** 2.4kg (Fully assembled), 1.5kg (Handheld configuration).
*   **Dust Bin Capacity:** 0.6L with "One-Touch" bottom-release emptying.

## 3. Filtration & Air Quality
The DD-VX2 features a 4-stage cyclonic separation system to ensure exhausted air is cleaner than the intake:
1.  **Primary Cyclone:** Separates large debris and hair.
2.  **Stainless Steel Mesh:** Blocks fine grit and sand.
3.  **Midi-Filter:** Protects the motor from micro-dust.
4.  **Post-Motor HEPA-Style Filter:** Traps 99.9% of particles as small as 0.3 microns, including allergens and dust mites.

## 4. Included Accessories
*   **Motorized Floor Head:** Features integrated LED headlights for spotting dust under furniture.
*   **2-in-1 Crevice Tool:** For tight corners and AC vent cleaning.
*   **Upholstery Brush:** For sofas, curtains, and car interiors.
*   **Wall-Mount Dock:** Securely holds the vacuum and two accessories while charging.

## 5. Maintenance & Care
To maintain peak suction power and battery health, follow these critical care steps:
*   **Filter Washing:** Rinse the HEPA-style filter under cold running water monthly. **Crucial:** Allow the filter to air-dry completely for at least **24 hours** before reinserting. Using a damp filter will cause permanent motor damage and void the warranty.
*   **Brush Roll Clearance:** Use a pair of scissors to remove tangled hair or threads from the motorized floor head weekly to prevent bearing seize.
*   **Battery Health:** Avoid leaving the vacuum on the charger for more than 48 hours. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight (especially during UAE summer months).

## 6. Warranty & Service
*   **Main Unit:** 1-year manufacturer warranty on the motor and electronics.
*   **Consumables:** Batteries and filters are considered wear-and-tear items (refer to **HH-51**).
*   **Replacements:** Genuine replacement batteries (**DD-BAT2**) and filter packs (**DD-FLT2**) are available at all HomeHaven stores.
