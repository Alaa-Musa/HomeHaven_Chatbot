# Product Detail Sheet — ClimateCare Thermostat CC-T1 (HH-58)

## 1. Overview
The **ClimateCare CC-T1** is a precision smart-climate controller designed to optimize cooling efficiency in UAE residences. By integrating advanced scheduling with real-time environmental sensing, the CC-T1 reduces energy consumption during peak summer months while maintaining a consistent comfort profile.

## 2. Key Features & Highlights
*   **Intelligent Scheduling:** Programmable 7-day cycles with up to 6 setpoints per day (e.g., Wake, Leave, Return, Sleep).
*   **Eco-Automation:** One-touch "Eco Mode" adjusts the setpoint to the most energy-efficient temperature (typically 24°C) when the home is unoccupied.
*   **Remote Management:** Full control via the **HomeHaven App**. Monitor home temperature and adjust cooling from anywhere.
*   **Precision Sensing:** Humidity and temperature sensors calibrated to ±0.5°C accuracy.

## 3. Connectivity & Requirements
*   **Network:** Compatible with **2.4GHz Wi-Fi** bands only. (Will not connect to 5GHz-only SSIDs).
*   **Power:** Requires a **C-Wire (Common Wire)** or a compatible 24V power adapter for continuous operation.
*   **HVAC Compatibility:** 
    *   **Supported:** Most centralized AC systems, heat pumps, and multi-stage cooling (2C/2H).
    *   **Not Supported:** High-voltage line systems (110V/220V), proprietary communicating thermostats, or certain district cooling (Chiller) valve actuators without a relay interface.

## 4. Installation & Setup
*   **Professional Recommendation:** Due to the complexity of HVAC wiring and the risk of short-circuiting the control board, **professional installation is strongly recommended**. 
*   **In-App Guide:** For DIY users, the app provides a step-by-step wiring terminal map based on your existing thermostat's wire colors.
*   **Mounting:** Includes a trim plate to cover holes or paint gaps from older, larger horizontal thermostats.

## 5. Maintenance & Support
*   **Firmware Updates:** Ensure the device remains connected to Wi-Fi to receive automatic "Over-the-Air" (OTA) security patches and feature enhancements.
*   **Calibration:** If the displayed temperature differs from a reference thermometer, use the "Temperature Offset" feature in the Advanced Settings menu.
*   **Battery Backup:** Internal coin-cell battery retains clock and schedule settings during brief power outages.
