The HomeHaven **AirPurifier Series** is engineered specifically for the UAE’s unique environmental challenges, including ultra-fine desert dust (PM2.5), high humidity, and seasonal allergens. This extended detail sheet compares the AP-200, AP-300, and AP-400 lines to help households select the optimal filtration volume.

## 3. Maintenance & Accessories

### Filter Replacement Packs
To maintain the AHAM-verified CADR ratings, only genuine HomeHaven filters should be used:
*   **AP-F200:** 2-in-1 Composite filter (Replace every 4–6 months).
*   **AP-F300:** 3-stage True HEPA drum (Replace every 6 months).
*   **AP-F400:** High-capacity H13 HEPA + Honeycomb Carbon (Replace every 6–8 months).

### Care Instructions
*   **Sensor Cleaning:** Every 30 days, wipe the laser dust sensor lens (located on the rear panel) with a dry cotton swab to ensure accurate "Auto Mode" adjustments.
*   **Pre-Filter Vacuuming:** For the AP-300 and AP-400, vacuum the permanent fabric pre-filter every 2 weeks to prevent "caking" from heavy dust.
*   **Plastic Removal:** **CRITICAL.** New filters ship inside protective plastic. Operating the unit without removing this plastic will cause the motor to overheat and void the warranty.

## 4. Warranty & Support
*   **Standard Term:** 1-year limited manufacturer warranty covering the DC motor and control board.
*   **Exclusions:** Clogged filters, physical damage to the chassis, or units used in commercial smoking environments.
*   **Troubleshooting:**
    *   **Red Ring Always On:** Indicates the sensor is dirty or the filter is at 0% life. Perform a sensor wipe and check the "Filter Reset" status.
    *   **Weak Airflow:** Check if the unit is placed too close to a wall. Maintain at least 30cm of clearance for the 360° intake.
    *   **Whistling Sound:** Ensure the bottom filter hatch is locked tight (HH-46).
