# Product Detail Sheet — PureBlend Blender PB-900 (HH-32)

## 1. Overview
The **PureBlend PB-900** is a high-torque 900W power blender engineered for the modern UAE kitchen. Designed to handle everything from frozen fruit smoothies to traditional lentil soups, the PB-900 utilizes a six-point stainless steel blade system to emulsify tough ingredients and crush ice into snow in seconds.

## 2. Key Specifications
*   **Motor Power:** 900W High-Efficiency Copper Motor (22,000 RPM peak).
*   **Jug Capacity:** **1.5L** BPA-Free Tritan™ Pitcher (Impact-resistant and thermal-shock safe).
*   **Blade Assembly:** Pro-Extractor 6-blade set (Titanium-coated for edge retention).
*   **Control Interface:** Precision rotary dial with blue LED illumination.
*   **Base:** Vibration-dampening suction feet for high-speed stability.

## 3. Operational Modes & Versatility
*   **Speed 1 (Low):** Ideal for soft fruits, protein shakes, and light mixing.
*   **Speed 2 (High):** Optimized for fibrous vegetables, nuts, and thick purées.
*   **Pulse Mode:** Provides manual control for chopping salsas or starting heavy loads.
*   **Ice-Crush Function:** A specialized interval pulse program designed to crack ice cubes without overheating the motor.

## 4. Safety & Thermal Protection
*   **Lid-Interlock System:** The PB-900 features a mechanical safety sensor; the motor will not engage unless the lid is fully seated and the locking tabs are clicked into place.
*   **Thermal Overload Protector (TOP):** Automatically cuts power if the internal motor temperature exceeds safe limits.
*   **Overheating Prevention (Duty Cycle):** To protect the motor's lifespan, do not run the unit continuously for more than **60 seconds**. Always allow the motor to rest for **2 minutes** between blending cycles.

## 5. Care & Maintenance
*   **Self-Cleaning Hack:** Fill the jug halfway with warm water and a drop of dish soap; run on Speed 1 for 30 seconds, then rinse.
*   **Blade Care:** The blade assembly is removable for deep cleaning. Always use a brush to clean around the blades; never use your bare hands.
*   **Dishwasher Safety:** The pitcher and lid are top-rack dishwasher safe. The motor base should only be wiped with a damp cloth.

## 6. Support & Warranty
*   **Standard Coverage:** 1-year limited warranty against motor and electrical failure.
*   **Common Issue:** If the unit stops during a heavy blend, it has likely tripped the thermal fuse. Unplug the unit and wait 30 minutes before pressing the "Reset" button on the bottom of the base.
